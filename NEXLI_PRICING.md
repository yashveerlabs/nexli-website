# NEXLI — PRICING (Internal Reference)

**Status:** Internal working model · finalized 2026-06-19
**Do NOT publish.** Website shows "Book a Demo / Contact for Pricing" only. Publish nothing until 2–3 paying reference schools exist. These numbers are an internal guide for quoting and negotiation.
**Currency:** INR. All prices are for the core Nexli platform (all features included). AI and WhatsApp/SMS are separate add-ons (below).

---

## 1. Founding Schools

- **₹25,000 flat for the first 6 months**, regardless of size = **₹4,166/month** (cheaper than every standard band's monthly rate).
- After 6 months, the founding school **converts to the normal band price** for its student count.
- **Founding cohort capped at ~2–3 schools at a time** (real cost ceiling = solo-founder support time, not server cost).
- Founding flat ₹25k applies to schools up to **~3,000 students**. For larger "founding" schools, give a **% discount off the standard rate** instead of the flat ₹25k (otherwise it's a giveaway).
- The founding price is a **Founding Partner deal**: discounted rate + personal setup & data migration + month-to-month exit + money-back, **in exchange for** a named case study, a testimonial, and 2 warm intros to other schools.
- **Add-ons (AI, WhatsApp/SMS) cost extra for founders too.**

## 3. Add-Ons (extra for every school, founders included)

### Nexli Intelligence (AI)
- **+30% of the base plan price**, billed on the same period as the base plan.
- AI compute is **usage-based**, so for heavy users or very large schools (10,000+) price it as **metered / custom**, not a flat 30%.
- Optional alternative model: **~₹20 per student per month**.

### WhatsApp / SMS
- **Metered pass-through, sold as prepaid credits** — NOT a flat markup (Meta and mobile operators charge per message).
- Indicative rates: **WhatsApp ≈ ₹0.35–1.00/message** (utility vs marketing), **SMS ≈ ₹0.20–0.30/message**.
- Optional **communication-platform fee ₹500–2,000/month** by school size to cover sending infrastructure.
- School tops up credits; bill at cost + small margin.

## 4. Standing Rules

1. **No lifetime plans.** Recurring server/API cost makes one-time pricing unsafe. Offerings = monthly / 3-month / 6-month / yearly / 5-year only.
2. **All core features on every plan.** Differentiation is by school size, not by locking features. AI and comms are the only paid add-ons.
3. **Annual (or longer) billing is the default ask** — matches the school financial year, reduces churn and payment overhead. Monthly is the convenience option at the higher effective rate.
4. **Bill founding schools by manual UPI / bank transfer + invoice now.** A payment gateway (Razorpay) + GST invoicing must be live before charging standard paying schools.
5. **Keep this file internal.** Do not put a price table on the website yet.

## 5. Reference — Indian School ERP Market (pulled 2026-06-19)

- Dominant model: **per-student, ₹100–500/student/year.** Flat subscriptions **₹25k–₹2L/year** by size.
- Small (≤500): ₹20k–75k/yr · Medium (500–1,500): ₹75k–2.5L/yr.
- Fedena: ~₹40k+/yr **plus** per-student fees, onboarding charged extra; example plan ₹150/student/yr.
- Budget disruptor (EduGradUP): ₹12k/yr flat unlimited + free migration + money-back.
- **Positioning:** Nexli competes on **premium quality / superior UX**, not price. Do not race the ₹12k player to the bottom. Bands above sit at the upper-mid of the market — raise the ceiling once references exist.
