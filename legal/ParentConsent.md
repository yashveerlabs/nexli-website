# Parental Consent for Processing Your Child's Personal Data

**School:** _[School to insert name, address and UDISE/affiliation details]_

This form asks for your consent, as a parent or legal guardian, to allow the School to collect and use your child's personal information through **Nexli**, the School's management software. We ask for this consent because the law (the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**, especially **section 9** on children's data) requires the School to obtain **verifiable consent from a parent or guardian** before processing a child's personal data.

Please read this carefully. You can ask the School any questions before signing.

---

## 1. Who is responsible for your child's data?

The **School is responsible** for deciding how your child's information is used (it is the "Data Fiduciary"). **Nexli** is the software provider that stores and processes the information **only on the School's instructions** (it is the "Data Processor"). Your child's data is intended to be stored in India.

## 2. What information we collect

Depending on the services your family uses, the School may collect:
- Your child's name, date of birth, gender, photograph, class/section, admission/roll number and address;
- Academic information: marks, report cards, attendance and progress;
- Health and emergency information: allergies, medical notes, immunisation, and emergency contacts;
- Transport details (only if your child uses school transport): route, stop and bus-location association;
- Communication details so we can reach you;
- Fee and payment records.

**We do not collect biometric data** (such as fingerprints or face scans). We do **not** use your child's data to show **targeted advertisements**, and we do **not** **track or monitor your child's behaviour** for advertising.

## 3. Why we use it (purposes)

We will use your child's personal data for these specific purposes:

| Purpose | What it is for |
|---|---|
| **Enrolment & records** | Admission, maintaining your child's student record. |
| **Academics** | Marks, report cards, assessments and progress. |
| **Attendance** | Recording daily attendance and informing you of absences. |
| **Health & medical / emergency** | Storing allergy/medical notes and contacting you in an emergency. |
| **Transport** | Sharing bus route, stop and live-location with the assigned guardian (only if you use transport). |
| **Communication** | Sending circulars, updates and notices to you. |
| **Fee processing** | Generating fee invoices and recording payments. |

Some purposes (for example, enrolment, core academic records, attendance, and emergency contact) are **necessary** for the School to admit and educate your child, and to comply with law. Other purposes are **optional** and you may decline them.

## 4. Your rights

You have the right to:
- **See** what information the School holds about your child;
- **Correct or update** information that is wrong or out of date;
- **Ask for erasure** (deletion) of information that is no longer needed, where the law does not require the School to keep it;
- **Withdraw your consent** for any optional purpose at any time; and
- **Raise a grievance** if you are unhappy with how the data is handled.

**Withdrawing consent:** You can withdraw consent for an optional purpose at any time by writing to the School. If you withdraw consent for a service, the School may no longer be able to provide that particular service to your child. Withdrawal does not undo processing that already lawfully took place.

## 5. How long we keep it

The School keeps your child's information only for as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law (for example, academic records that schools must keep). After that, the information is deleted or anonymised.
