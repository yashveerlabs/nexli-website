## Executive Summary

Effective school management requires excellence across people, finances, and operations. Schools with strong management systems report 70% better outcomes and higher stakeholder satisfaction. This article covers key management areas and best practices.

---

## Key Principles

- **Clear systems:** Transparent processes reduce confusion and conflict
- **Accountability:** Everyone knows their responsibilities and is held accountable
- **Data-driven:** Decisions based on facts, not assumptions
- **Continuous improvement:** Always looking to improve
- **Staff development:** Invest in people
- **Financial health:** Sustainable business model
- **Stakeholder trust:** Parents and staff have confidence in leadership

---

## Core Management Areas

### 1. People Management
- Staff selection and development
- Clear expectations and feedback
- Recognition and retention
- Culture building

### 2. Financial Management
- Budget planning and control
- Fee collection and financial forecasting
- Cost management without compromising quality
- Financial reporting to board

### 3. Operational Management
- Clear processes and workflows
- Efficient systems
- Quality infrastructure
- Regulatory compliance

### 4. Academic Management
- Curriculum oversight
- Teaching quality monitoring
- Student learning assessment
- Continuous academic improvement
