## Executive Summary

Your school's quality depends on your leadership team. Schools with strong teams report 70% better results and faster execution. This article covers identifying, hiring, and developing leaders.

---

## Key Roles

**VP:** Manages major function; represents principal

**Academic Coordinator:** Manages curriculum and teaching quality

**Finance Manager:** Manages budget and fees

**Admin Coordinator:** Manages operations

**HOD:** Manages department; supervises teachers

---

## Hiring Leadership

### Criteria
- Subject expertise
- Management experience
- Values alignment
- Good judgment
- Stability and respect from staff

### Process
1. Define role clearly
2. Internal candidates first
3. External candidates
4. Interviews with principal + relevant staff
5. Reference checks
6. Hire based on fit

---

## Developing Leaders

### Ongoing Development
- Monthly 1-on-1 coaching
- Annual training/conferences
- Peer learning visits
- Stretch project delegation

### Culture
- Psychological safety
- Collaborative problem-solving
- Continuous improvement
