## Executive Summary

Schools that understand their competitive landscape report 50% better enrollment growth and stronger market positioning. Competitive analysis identifies threats, opportunities, and your unique differentiators. This article teaches how to analyze competitors and use insights strategically.

---

## Step 1: Identify Competitors

**Direct competitors:**
- Schools in your area serving similar socioeconomic group
- Schools with same curriculum (CBSE, ICSE, etc.)
- Schools offering same programs

**Indirect competitors:**
- Schools in nearby areas
- Online/hybrid schools
- Coaching centers (for academics, sports, arts)

**List 5-10 main competitors.**

---

## Step 2: Research Competitors

For each, gather:
- **Academics:** Pass rates, board rankings, special programs (coding, sports, arts)
- **Infrastructure:** Facilities, tech, labs
- **Fees:** Amount, structure, payment flexibility
- **Programs:** Unique offerings, strengths
- **Staff:** Reputation, experience
- **Enrollment:** Student numbers, growth rate
- **Parent satisfaction:** Reviews, word-of-mouth, retention

---

## Step 3: Competitive Matrix

| Factor | Your School | Competitor A | Competitor B | Competitor C |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fees | ₹2.5L | ₹3L | ₹2L | ₹2.8L |
| Pass Rate | 82% | 85% | 75% | 80% |
| Science Lab | Moderate | Excellent | Basic | Good |
| Coding Program | No | Yes | No | Yes |
| Sports | Good | Average | Excellent | Good |
| Boarding | No | Yes | Yes | No |
| Parent Rating | 4.2/5 | 4.5/5 | 3.8/5 | 4.3/5 |

This shows where you're strong, where you're weak, where market demand is.

---

## Step 4: Identify Your Differentiation

Based on analysis:
- **What are you better at than competitors?** (Science, sports, inclusion, affordability)
- **What gap exists in the market?** (No affordable school with good Science? Opportunity.)
- **What's your unique value?** (Only school in area with coding program? That's differentiation.)

---

## Step 5: Strategic Response

If **competitor is better at something important:**
- Match or exceed (invest)
- Or differentiate elsewhere

If **you have unique strengths:**
- Amplify and market
- Defend against new entrants

If **market gap exists:**
- Launch new program/focus
- Market aggressively
