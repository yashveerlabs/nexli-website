## Executive Summary

Schools with structured annual planning cycles report 65% better goal achievement and smoother operations. Reactive schools (no plan) waste time, repeat mistakes, and miss opportunities. This article provides a calendar for systematic annual planning.

---

## Annual Planning Calendar

### June-July: Strategy & Budget
- Review prior year results
- Strategic priorities for the coming year
- Budget allocation
- Staffing needs

### July-August: Hiring & Preparation
- Recruit and hire staff
- Curriculum preparation
- Infrastructure maintenance
- Planning committees

### August-September: Onboarding & Launch
- Staff onboarding
- Academic year kick-off
- Student admissions
- Events planning

### September-April: Execution & Monitoring
- Regular monitoring
- Monthly reviews
- Adjustments as needed
- Progress tracking

### April-May: Annual Review & Planning
- Year-end results assessment
- Staff and parent feedback
- Learning for next year
- Begin next cycle planning

---

## Month-by-Month Detail

**June:**
- Board meeting: Review prior year performance
- Academic review: Pass rates, enrollment, key metrics
- Financial review: Revenue, expenses, margins
- Strategic priorities for next year: (Academic focus, operational improvements, growth)

**July:**
- Budget finalized and approved
- Hiring for vacancies completed
- Curriculum finalized
- Calendar published

**August:**
- Staff onboarding and training
- Infrastructure ready
- Admissions process running
- Events planned

**September:**
- Academic year begins
- Monitoring systems activated
- First month review

**October-March:**
- Quarterly reviews (Oct, Jan, April)
- Adjustments as needed
- Tracking against plan

**April:**
- Final results in
- Year-end assessment
- Staff feedback gathering

**May:**
- Annual report prepared
- Board presentation
- Next year planning begins
