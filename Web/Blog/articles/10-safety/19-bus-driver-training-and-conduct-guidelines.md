# Bus Driver Training and Conduct Guidelines

Professional bus drivers ensure safe transportation. Training and conduct standards are essential.

**Initial Training** - School procedures, routes, student names, special needs, emergency protocols, behavior expectations, technology systems, safety procedures.

**Ongoing Training** - Annual refresher, new procedures, safety updates, customer service, crisis response, professional development, continuous learning.

**Safety Training** - Vehicle maintenance, emergency procedures, first aid/CPR, defensive driving, hazard recognition, incident response, prevention focus.

**Student Management** - Appropriate behavior with students, professionalism, boundaries, language acceptable, confidentiality, child safety, dignity.

**Communication** - Parent communication, school staff collaboration, incident reporting, clarity on procedures, accessibility to information, transparent processes.

**Conduct Standards** - Professional demeanor, arrival on time, vehicle maintenance, appropriate language, no discrimination, respect for all, ethical behavior.

**Accountability** - Performance monitoring, incident tracking, feedback regular, evaluation annual, consequences for violations, fairness, expectations clear.

**Discipline** - Progressive discipline for violations, warning first, suspension if serious, termination for egregious, documentation thorough, fairness maintained.

**Recognition** - Safe driving recognition, excellent service awards, retention incentives, appreciation expressed, professionalism valued, morale building.

**Mental Health** - Job stress acknowledged, support available, EAP if provided, wellness programs, camaraderie, team culture, burnout prevention.

**Diversity and Inclusion** - Diverse hiring, cultural competence, language support, inclusive environment, representation, belonging for all.

**Continuing Education** - Relevant training opportunities, professional development, skill building, updating knowledge, career development, investment in drivers.

Professional standards ensure safe, quality transportation for all students.
