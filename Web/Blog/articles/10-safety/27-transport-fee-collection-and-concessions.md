# Transport Fee Collection and Concessions

Transportation fees need fair collection policies and concessions for low-income families.

**Fee Structure** - Clear fees established, payment terms, monthly or annual, online payment options, flexibility possible.

**Affordability** - Fees reasonable, low-income concessions, scholarships available, sliding scale possible, equity access, transportation justice.

**Concessions** - Reduced fees for low-income families, free for qualifying, documentation process, confidentiality maintained, dignity preserved.

**Payment Options** - Monthly installments, annual discounts, payment plans, flexible schedule, cash, card, online options.

**Collection Process** - Notices sent, reminders provided, payment expectations clear, procedures fair, compassion for hardship.

**Delinquency Process** - Follow-up on unpaid fees, payment plans offered, suspension only last resort, family communication, solutions-focused.

**Financial Hardship** - Compassionate process, conversation about barriers, assistance offered, services not cut off immediately, problem-solving.

**Communication** - Fees clearly explained, payment options known, hardship process understood, transparency, accessibility.

**Equity** - All students served regardless of ability to pay, equitable access, funding for concessions, transportation provided.

**Budget** - Fee revenue projected, concessions budgeted, affordability analysis, sustainability planned, long-term financial planning.

**Reporting** - Collection rates tracked, fee revenue reported, expenditures documented, financial accountability, transparent reporting.

Fair fee collection ensures transportation services are accessible to all students.
