# Vehicle Maintenance Schedule: School Buses

Regular maintenance ensures bus safety and longevity. Preventive maintenance saves money and prevents breakdowns.

**Daily Checks** - Driver inspection, exterior, tires, lights, fluid levels, brakes, emergency equipment, documentation logged, issues reported immediately.

**Weekly Maintenance** - Fluid topped off, tire pressure checked, battery connections, all systems functioning, documentation, systematic approach.

**Monthly Service** - Comprehensive inspection, fluid replacement, filter checks, brake pads, belts, hoses, lights tested, mechanical systems validated.

**Quarterly Maintenance** - Deep inspection, transmission fluid, coolant, all filters, battery, alignment, emission checks, comprehensive service.

**Annual Inspections** - Full mechanical inspection, emissions testing, safety certification, brake system complete, suspension, all systems validated, records maintained.

**Maintenance Contracts** - Qualified vendor selected, service schedule, cost predictability, quality assured, communication regular, coordination efficient.

**Documentation** - Maintenance log, service records, repairs completed, parts replaced, technician notes, parts supplier, cost tracking, trend analysis.

**Breakdown Response** - Immediate response if breakdown, student safety priority, alternative transportation arranged, families notified, incident tracked.

**Replacement Planning** - Vehicles have lifespan, age tracking, replacement budget, funding secured, planned transitions, continuous fleet updates.

**Driver Reporting** - Drivers report problems immediately, communication system, escalation clear, concerns taken seriously, quick response.

**Compliance** - Safety standards met, regulations followed, inspections current, certifications valid, legal compliance, insurance requirements.

**Budget** - Maintenance costs projected, preventive better than reactive, long-term planning, resource allocation, sustainability.

Regular maintenance prevents emergencies and ensures bus fleet safety and reliability.
