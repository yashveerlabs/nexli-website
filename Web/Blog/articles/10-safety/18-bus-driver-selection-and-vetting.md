# Bus Driver Selection and Vetting

Bus drivers are student caretakers. Thorough vetting ensures trustworthiness.

**Qualifications** - Commercial license required, appropriate class, medical certification current, background clearance, experience preferred.

**Background Check** - Criminal record check, driving record clean, sex offender registry screening, reference checks, thorough vetting, no surprises.

**Driving Record** - No serious violations, safety focus, insurance record, accident history, driver rating, driving judgment demonstrated.

**Testing** - Driving test required, skill assessment, mechanical knowledge, student interaction simulation, temperament, suitability evaluation.

**Interviews** - Structured interviews, scenario questions, motivation assessed, compatibility with school values, professionalism, background verified.

**Reference Checks** - Previous employers contacted, reliability confirmed, any concerns noted, integrity checked, performance history.

**Medical Certification** - Physicals current, medications reviewed, limitations identified, vision and hearing tested, medical fitness confirmed.

**Training** - Pre-employment training, school procedures, student management, emergency protocols, safety procedures, initial orientation.

**Continuous Screening** - Annual driving record checks, criminal record monitoring, license verification, ongoing compliance, continuous vetting.

**Hiring Decision** - Best candidates selected, multiple qualified applicants, team recommendation, principal approval, offer and contract.

**Onboarding** - Orientation comprehensive, mentor assignment, community tour, student introduction, culture integration, smooth beginning.

**Compensation** - Competitive wages, benefits, retention focus, recognition, professionalism supported, appropriate pay.

Thorough driver selection and vetting protects student safety and ensures quality transportation.
