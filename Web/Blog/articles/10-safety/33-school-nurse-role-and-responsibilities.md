# School Nurse: Role and Responsibilities

School nurses provide essential healthcare and health promotion. Clear role definition ensures effective service.

**Clinical Care** - First aid, medications, health assessments, wound care, emergency response, medical procedures, health monitoring.

**Health Education** - Disease prevention, health promotion, wellness programs, age-appropriate education, parent education, community health.

**Mental Health Support** - Referrals to counseling, mental health awareness, crisis response, supportive relationships, resources.

**Special Needs** - Care plans, specialized care, mobility assistance, specialized equipment, IEP/504 coordination, advocacy.

**Immunization** - Record verification, education, referrals for vaccines, compliance, documentation, communicable disease prevention.

**Medication Management** - Secure storage, dispensing, records, education, safety, controlled substances if applicable, documentation.

**Screening** - Vision, hearing, dental, development, health status, referrals, documentation, pattern identification.

**Health Records** - Maintained properly, secure, confidential, accessible to authorized parties, family access, compliance.

**Communication** - Parents, teachers, doctors, specialists, collaborative care, professional relationships, information sharing appropriate.

**Emergency Response** - Trained in protocols, 911 procedures, communicating with parents, transport, incident documentation.

**Community Partnerships** - Public health agencies, medical providers, mental health, specialized services, referral network, comprehensive care.

**Professional Development** - Continuing education, certifications current, training on new procedures, specialty certification, expertise maintenance.

**Administrative** - Record-keeping, compliance documentation, reporting required, budget management, supply ordering, organization.

**Leadership** - Advocating for student health, health policy development, staff training, program evaluation, visibility, commitment.

Effective school nurses provide comprehensive healthcare and promote student wellness.
