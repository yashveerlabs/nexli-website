# Security Guards: Hiring and Training

Security guards are first line of school defense. Hire and train carefully.

**Hiring** - Background checks thorough, experience required, customer service skills, conflict de-escalation training, certifications needed, vetting careful.

**Qualifications** - Security certification preferred, physical fitness, communication skills, judgment and discernment, authority respect, reliability.

**Training** - School procedures learned, emergency protocols, technology systems, student interaction, parent recognition, boundaries understood, ongoing training.

**Responsibilities** - Entry monitoring, visitor screening, facility patrols, emergency response, incident reporting, documentation, communication with administration.

**Student Interaction** - Friendly and approachable, authority balanced with care, student rapport, modeling behavior, mentoring role possible.

**Technology** - ID badge systems, camera monitoring, access control, communication systems, incident logging, data security, system proficiency.

**Communication** - Regular updates with administration, incident reports, concerns escalated, feedback provided, collaboration, team approach.

**Evaluation** - Performance review regular, incident response effectiveness, community feedback, professional development, compensation appropriate.

**Mental Health** - Job stress acknowledged, support available, counseling if needed, team culture positive, appreciation shown, wellness priority.

**Diversity** - Reflect school community, cultural competence, language diversity, inclusive hiring, representation matters.

**Accountability** - Clear expectations, performance standards, consequences for violations, transparent process, fairness, professional conduct.

Quality security staff enhance safety while maintaining welcoming school environment.
