# Bike and Scooter Storage: School Responsibility

Secure bike/scooter storage encourages active transportation. School provides safe storage.

**Storage Facility** - Covered racks, secure location, adequate space, weather protection, organized, accessible design, capacity sufficient.

**Security** - Lock provisions, surveillance if possible, theft prevention, accountability, insurance coverage, incident response.

**Maintenance** - Facility kept clean, repairs addressed, organization systems, regular inspection, upkeep consistent.

**Rules and Procedures** - Registration required, rules explained, consequences for violations, student responsibility, shared care.

**Access** - During school hours secured, after-school availability, weekend access possible, emergency procedures, flexibility.

**Liability** - Insurance coverage, release forms, damage protocols, theft responsibility limits, legal protections.

**Student Education** - Bike/scooter safety training, helmet use, road safety, responsibility, safe storage habits, citizenship.

**Family Communication** - Procedures explained, expectations clear, safety emphasized, responsibility outlined, engagement encouraged.

**Maintenance Standards** - Equipment in good repair, safety checks, removal of hazardous items, cleanliness, organization.

**Alternative Transportation** - Encourages walking/biking, environmental benefits, health benefits, independence, sustainability.

**Monitoring** - Regular checks, theft reporting, damage investigation, rule enforcement, accountability maintenance.

**Continuous Improvement** - Feedback collected, improvements made, capacity expansion if needed, safety enhancements, best practices.

Secure bike/scooter storage supports active, sustainable transportation for students.
