# School Medical Clinic: Setup and Operations

School medical clinic provides essential healthcare. Proper setup and operations ensure quality care.

**Facility** - Clinic space, equipment, supplies, cleanliness, confidentiality, accessibility, comfortable environment, safety standards.

**Staffing** - Nurse(s), medical professionals, first aid trained, on-site or visiting, adequate coverage, qualifications, expertise.

**Hours** - Full-day coverage, limited hours, emergency response, student access, during/after school, availability clear.

**Services** - First aid, medication administration, health screening, wound care, emergency response, referral services, health education.

**Equipment** - Standard medical equipment, medications stock, first aid supplies, emergency equipment, blood pressure cuff, thermometer, defibrillator.

**Records** - Medical records maintained, privacy protected, confidentiality, family access, secure storage, compliance with regulations.

**Medication Management** - Secure storage, refrigeration if needed, controlled dispensing, record-keeping, labeling, safety protocols.

**Allergy Information** - Documented, accessible, emergency plans, staff awareness, emergency medications, communication, action plans.

**Health Screening** - Vision, hearing, dental checks, immunization verification, health assessments, referrals as needed, documentation.

**Emergency Response** - Protocols for emergencies, 911 procedures, communication to parents, transport arrangements, incident documentation.

**Health Education** - Wellness programs, disease prevention, healthy habits, sexual health, mental health, age-appropriate education.

**Collaboration** - With teachers, parents, specialists, community health, comprehensive approach, coordination, integrated services.

**Data** - Tracking health issues, trends identified, follow-up documentation, reporting required, privacy maintained, analysis for improvement.

**Compliance** - Licensure current, regulations followed, standards met, inspections passed, documentation, legal requirements.

School medical clinics provide essential healthcare access and preventive services.
