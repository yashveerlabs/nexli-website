# Transport Enrollment: Managing Student Pickup

Transportation enrollment affects operations. Manage carefully for efficiency and equity.

**Enrollment Process** - Form completed, address verified, contact info, pickup/dropoff locations, special needs, emergency contacts, student ID assigned.

**Verification** - Address confirmed, service area eligibility, walking distance assessed, need determined, appropriateness evaluated, fairness ensured.

**Pick-up/Dropoff** - Designated locations, safe locations, walking distance reasonable, inclement weather consideration, multiple options where possible.

**Special Needs** - Students with disabilities accommodated, mobility assistance, seat accommodations, aide availability, individualized plans, inclusion.

**Fee Structure** - Free transportation if offered, paid option if available, affordability, equity access, fee waivers for low-income, transportation justice.

**Cancellations** - Change process simple, notification procedures, refund procedures, accommodation for changes, flexibility, responsiveness.

**Communication** - Schedule provided, route information, pickup times, contact methods, regular updates, emergency notifications, clear procedures.

**Capacity Management** - Waiting list if at capacity, priority criteria, additions as possible, communication about limits, fairness in allocation.

**Attendance Monitoring** - Pickup coordination with attendance, excused absences, home visits if needed, engagement support, attendance importance.

**Emergency Information** - Contacts updated, emergency procedures, special medical needs, accessibility requirements, safety information, regular review.

**Data Management** - Enrollment records, address database, efficiency metrics, cost analysis, annual review, continuous improvement.

Well-managed transportation enrollment serves students equitably and efficiently.
