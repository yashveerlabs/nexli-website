# Transport Policy for Parents

Clear transport policies inform parents and prevent misunderstandings.

**Eligibility** - Who qualifies for transportation, distance criteria, within service area, special circumstances, clear communication.

**Routes and Schedules** - Published routes, pickup times, drop-off procedures, schedule reliability, communication of changes.

**Fees and Payment** - Fees explained, payment procedures, financial hardship assistance, concessions available, affordability.

**Student Conduct** - Expectations clear, behavior standards, consequences for misbehavior, safety rules, professionalism required.

**Parental Responsibility** - Punctuality, notification of changes, communication with school, damage responsibility, partnership role.

**Cancellations and Changes** - Procedure for canceling service, changing locations, notification requirements, refund policies.

**Safety Procedures** - Emergency procedures, pickup protocols, parent communication, incident response, student accountability.

**Special Needs** - Accommodations available, application process, services provided, dignity maintained, inclusivity commitment.

**Complaints and Resolution** - How to file complaints, response timeframe, investigation procedures, resolution process, escalation available.

**Emergency Procedures** - Evacuation procedures, severe weather, lockdown, parent notification, student care, crisis management.

**Technology Use** - Tracking system use, privacy policies, app access, communication systems, data security.

**Community Standards** - Respect for drivers/staff, no harassment, professional conduct, shared responsibility, community culture.

**Updates** - Annual review, changes communicated, flexibility shown, continuous improvement, feedback welcomed.

Clear policies ensure parental understanding and appropriate transportation service.
