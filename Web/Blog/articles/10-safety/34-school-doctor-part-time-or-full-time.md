# School Doctor: Part-Time or Full-Time

School doctors provide clinical expertise. Decision between part-time and full-time depends on needs.

**Full-Time Doctor** - On-site daily, complex medical cases, specialized care, medical decision-making, consultations, continuous presence, full capacity.

**Part-Time Doctor** - Visiting regularly, consultation available, complex cases, specific days, availability scheduled, cost-effective, specialized expertise.

**Staffing Options** - Employed by school, contracted with medical service, shared with district, volunteer, multiple part-time, flexible models.

**Roles** - Medical consultations, IEP/504 participation, staff training, health screenings, medication oversight, emergency response, health promotion.

**Qualifications** - MD or DO, pediatric background, school health knowledge, licensure current, experience valuable, certification preferred.

**Responsibilities** - Oversee clinic operations, supervise nurse, approve medications, complex case consultation, medical decisions, record review, compliance.

**Collaboration** - Works with school nurse, communicates with parents, consults specialists, coordinates care, collaborative model, integrated approach.

**Training** - Staff training on medical procedures, parent education, student education, professional development, knowledge sharing, competency.

**Emergency Response** - Protocol leadership, emergency procedures, advanced care, crisis decisions, communication, accountability.

**Data and Reporting** - Health trends, documentation, statistical analysis, reporting to administration, regulatory compliance, improvement recommendations.

**Community Health** - Public health initiatives, vaccination drives, disease prevention, health education, community partnerships, leadership.

**Budget Considerations** - Salary/contract costs, clinic expenses, specialist referrals, supplies, ROI analysis, funding sustainability, cost-effectiveness.

**Evaluation** - Performance assessment, staff feedback, student health outcomes, efficiency, satisfaction, continuous improvement, accountability.

School medical oversight ensures comprehensive healthcare and compliance with regulations.
