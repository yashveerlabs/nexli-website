# Allergies and Chronic Conditions: Student Profile

Allergies and chronic conditions require careful management. Documentation ensures safe care.

**Documentation** - Forms completed, parent provided, symptoms, treatments, triggers, severity levels, emergency procedures, physician contact.

**Severe Allergies** - Food allergies, environmental allergies, insect allergies, medication allergies, anaphylaxis risk, emergency medications, action plans.

**Chronic Conditions** - Asthma, diabetes, seizure disorder, heart conditions, autoimmune conditions, management, medications, limitations.

**Emergency Medications** - EpiPen storage, inhaler access, seizure medications, other emergency meds, availability, staff training, usage protocols.

**Staff Awareness** - All relevant staff trained, food service aware, teachers understand, PE knows limitations, bus drivers informed, comprehensive knowledge.

**Accommodation Plans** - Diet accommodations, activity modifications, rest needs, equipment, monitoring, safe participation, inclusion, dignity.

**Action Plans** - Documented procedures, symptom recognition, medication administration, family notification, emergency response, clarity of steps.

**Monitoring** - Regular check-ins, medication effectiveness, new symptoms, changing needs, plan adjustments, communication with family.

**Peer Education** - Age-appropriate awareness, bullying prevention, inclusion support, understanding, empathy, community education.

**Community** - Restaurants nearby trained, emergency services accessible, hospitals, healthcare providers, safety network, community preparedness.

**Participation** - Field trips, sports, events, accommodations provided, inclusion maximized, dignity maintained, restrictions necessary only.

**Transitions** - Grade level transitions, new teachers, summer programs, consistent documentation, information transfer, continuity.

**Family Partnership** - Regular communication, plan updates, new information, changes implemented, family input, collaborative care.

Comprehensive allergy/condition documentation ensures student safety and appropriate care.

## How Nexli Manages Allergen and Condition Profiles

Nexli's Medical module stores allergen flags against every student's profile with severity rating and emergency protocol. Teachers opening the class roster see a health-flag indicator for students with severe allergies, prompting awareness without exposing full medical details. Canteen and hostel staff with appropriate roles can see allergen information relevant to food service. When a student with a known condition is sent to the clinic, the nurse sees the full profile immediately.
