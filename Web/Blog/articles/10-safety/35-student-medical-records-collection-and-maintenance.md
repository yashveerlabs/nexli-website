# Student Medical Records: Collection and Maintenance

Medical records are essential for safe student care. Proper collection and maintenance ensure access and compliance.

**Information Collected** - Health history, medications, allergies, emergency contacts, physician information, immunization records, special healthcare needs.

**Collection Methods** - Forms provided, physical exams, interviews, documentation gathered, updated annually, accuracy verified.

**Storage** - Secure location, confidential, organized, accessible to authorized personnel only, paper and electronic options, backup systems.

**Access** - Only authorized staff, purpose-driven access, parent access upon request, student privacy protected, confidentiality maintained, legal requirements.

**Privacy** - HIPAA compliance, data security, encryption, password protection, limited access, regular updates, privacy awareness training.

**Maintenance** - Current information, annual updates, review for accuracy, corrections made, cleanliness, organization, completeness.

**Emergency Information** - Clearly marked, quick access during emergency, essential information highlighted, regular verification, currency maintained.

**Allergies** - Prominently documented, all types listed, severity noted, visual alerts, staff awareness, emergency protocols, medication available.

**Medications** - Listed with dosage, frequency, prescribing physician, side effects, contraindications, proper storage, expiration dates, monitoring.

**Special Needs** - Documented comprehensively, accommodations specified, emergency procedures, staff training, equipment needs, communication.

**Purging Policy** - Records retained appropriately, graduation handling, retention schedule, secure destruction, legal requirements met, compliance.

**Transfer** - When student transfers, records transferred securely, family authorization, receiving school notification, continuity, appropriate communication.

**Compliance** - Legal requirements met, regulations followed, inspections passed, confidentiality maintained, documentation, accountability.

Comprehensive medical records ensure student safety and emergency preparedness.
