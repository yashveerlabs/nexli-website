## Annual Price Increases: What's Normal?

Understanding annual price increases: what's normal? is crucial for principals and administrators planning ERP implementation.

### The Problem

School budgets are tight. Technology investments compete with salaries, infrastructure, and educational programs. Without clear understanding of annual price increases: what's normal?, schools either overpay or choose inadequate systems.

### Consequences

- **Budget Overruns:** Hidden costs emerge during implementation.
- **Abandoned Implementations:** Systems remain underutilized due to inadequate training budget.
- **Vendor Pressure:** Without clear cost understanding, principals struggle to negotiate fairly.
- **False Economies:** Choosing cheap systems creates high support costs.

### Understanding the Costs

**Implementation Costs Include:**
- Software licensing
- Data migration
- Staff training
- Hardware and infrastructure
- Customization for board requirements
- Integration with existing systems

**Ongoing Costs Include:**
- Annual subscription
- Cloud infrastructure
- Support and updates
- Training for new staff
- System optimization

### ROI Calculation Framework

**Time Savings**
Calculate admin hours saved per week multiplied by blended hourly cost.

**Fee Collection Improvement**
If current collection is 85% and ERP improves it to 92%, calculate additional revenue.

**Operational Efficiency**
Reduced paper, printing, and manual process overhead.
