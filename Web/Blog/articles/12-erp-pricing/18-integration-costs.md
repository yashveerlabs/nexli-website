**Snippet:** ERP integration with existing systems (bank gateways, accounting software, transport apps) costs ₹50,000-₹2,00,000 per integration. Many schools have 3-5 integrations, totaling ₹2-8 lakhs. Choose ERPs with pre-built integrations to minimize custom development.

## Why Integrations Cost Money

Your school doesn't operate in isolation. You use:
- **Bank:** Fee payments collected online must flow automatically into ERP
- **Accounting software:** Tally or QuickBooks tracks financial ledger; ERP must sync
- **Transport:** Bus tracking app must know which students are on-board; ERP needs that data
- **Library:** Library software tracks issued books; ERP should show student's reading record
- **Communication:** WhatsApp or SMS gateway sends notifications; ERP needs integration for automation

Each integration requires API work: building secure data connections, mapping fields, error handling, testing.

## Integration Cost Breakdown

### Pre-Built Integrations (₹0-₹50,000)
Vendor already built integration with common systems.

**Examples:**
- Bank payment gateways (Razorpay, Instamojo, Cashfree): Usually free or ₹10,000-₹20,000 to set up
- WhatsApp integration: Usually free or ₹20,000-₹40,000
- GST-compliant accounting sync (Tally): Usually free
- Gmail/Google Workspace: Usually free

**Why cheap:** Vendor invested in building once; amortized cost across many customers.

**Vendor's motivation:** Pre-built integrations increase market adoption. Common integrations are worth the engineering time.

### Custom Integrations (₹50,000-₹3,00,000)
You need specific integration vendor doesn't support.

**Examples:**
- Integration with niche transport app (not national player)
- Integration with specialized library system
- Integration with custom HRMS used by your group
- Specialized accounting software (non-standard)

**Cost drivers:**
- Complexity: Simple data sync (₹50,000-₹1,00,000) vs. complex bidirectional logic (₹1,50,000-₹3,00,000)
- API documentation: Well-documented API (cheaper) vs. undocumented or proprietary (expensive, requires reverse-engineering)
- Data volume: Small sync (₹50,000) vs. bulk historical data sync (₹1,00,000+)
- Maintenance: Ongoing support and updates (add 20-30% to initial cost annually)

### No Integration (₹0, but adds manual work)
Manual workaround: Export data from one system, import into another.

**Cost:** No direct cost, but:
- Staff time to manually export/import weekly (10-20 hours = ₹5,000-₹10,000/month)
- Error risk: Manual entry creates duplicates, typos, mismatches
- Delay: Data syncs weekly or monthly, not real-time

**Over 5 years,** manual sync (₹5K-10K/month) costs ₹3-6 lakhs + error costs.

**Lesson:** If integration costs ₹1,50,000 upfront but saves ₹5,000/month in manual work, it pays for itself in 30 months. Most integrations are cost-justified.

## Common School Integrations & Costs

### 1. **Bank Payment Gateway Integration**
**Setup:** Fees paid online (UPI, debit card, net banking) automatically recorded in ERP.

**Vendors:** Razorpay, Instamojo, Cashfree, PayU
- Pre-built integration: ₹0-₹20,000 (setup + configuration)
- Custom integration: ₹50,000-₹1,00,000 (if vendor doesn't support your bank)

**Importance:** High. Without integration, finance staff manually logs each payment. 10-20 daily payments = 30 minutes manual work/day.

### 2. **Accounting Software Sync (Tally/QuickBooks)**
**Setup:** Fee data syncs to accounting software automatically.

**Cost:**
- Pre-built (Nexli ↔ Tally): ₹0-₹50,000
- Custom (Nexli ↔ custom accounting app): ₹1,00,000-₹2,00,000

**Importance:** High. Without integration, finance team manually enters fee data twice (once in ERP, once in accounting software). Double entry = error risk + time waste.

### 3. **Transport/Fleet Management**
**Setup:** Real-time GPS, student boarding/alighting data syncs to ERP.

**Cost:**
- Pre-built (Nexli ↔ common transport app): ₹50,000-₹1,00,000
- Custom (Nexli ↔ niche transport software): ₹1,50,000-₹3,00,000

**Importance:** Medium-High. Without integration, transport staff manually marks attendance. With integration, automatic.

### 4. **Library Management System**
**Setup:** Books issued/returned data syncs to ERP.

**Cost:**
- Pre-built: ₹30,000-₹75,000
- Custom: ₹75,000-₹1,50,000

**Importance:** Low-Medium. Nice-to-have. Students see their reading record in ERP portal. Not operational necessity.

### 5. **Biometric Attendance Devices**
**Setup:** Biometric attendance hardware syncs with ERP.

**Cost:**
- Pre-built (Nexli ↔ common biometric devices): ₹0-₹50,000
- Custom (Nexli ↔ niche biometric): ₹50,000-₹1,50,000

**Importance:** High. Without integration, staff manually enters attendance from biometric device. With integration, one-click sync.

### 6. **WhatsApp/SMS Gateway**
**Setup:** ERP can send automated SMS/WhatsApp notifications (fee reminders, attendance alerts, events).

**Cost:**
- Pre-built: ₹0-₹30,000
- Custom: ₹50,000-₹1,00,000

**Importance:** Medium. Notifications drive parent engagement. Without integration, staff manually sends via WhatsApp/SMS.

### 7. **Video Conferencing (Zoom, Google Meet)**
**Setup:** Class recordings stored in ERP for later access.

**Cost:**
- Pre-built: ₹20,000-₹50,000
- Custom: ₹75,000-₹1,50,000

**Importance:** Low. Optional. Enhances LMS functionality but not essential.

### 8. **Email System (Gmail/Outlook)**
**Setup:** ERP sends emails via your school's mail server (not generic mail).

**Cost:**
- Pre-built: ₹0-₹20,000
- Custom: ₹30,000-₹75,000

**Importance:** Medium. Ensures emails appear from school domain, not generic vendor domain.

## How to Reduce Integration Costs

### 1. **Choose ERP with Most Pre-Built Integrations**
Research vendors:
- How many integrations are pre-built?
- Are YOUR specific systems supported?
- Do pre-built integrations cover 80%+ of your needs?

Choosing ERP with 10 pre-built integrations saves ₹5-10 lakhs vs. vendor with 0 pre-built integrations.

### 2. **Prioritize Integrations**
Essential (go-live): Bank, Accounting = ₹1,50,000
Nice-to-have (Phase 2, after 6 months): Others = ₹2,00,000+

Don't force all integrations upfront. Phase them.

### 3. **Standardize on Common Systems**
Don't use niche software if standard option exists. Example:
- Use Razorpay (common) instead of custom bank API (niche) = ₹20,000 vs. ₹1,50,000 saved

### 4. **Use Data Export Instead of APIs**
Some integrations don't need real-time sync. Batch daily/weekly export works fine.
- Cost: ₹0-₹50,000 (simple scheduled export)
- vs. Real-time API: ₹1,00,000-₹2,00,000

### 5. **Vendor-Managed Integrations**
Ask vendor: "Do you manage integration on your end, or do we?" Vendor-managed (at their cost) is cheaper than vendor-supported (at your cost).

## Red Flags in Integration Quotes

1. **"We'll integrate with anything for ₹2,00,000."** Too vague. Integration complexity varies. Push for detailed scope and fixed price.

2. **"Integration will take 4 weeks because system X is hard."** If system X is hard, it should be complex + expensive. Question why timeline is standard for all integrations.

3. **"We can't guarantee integration success because it's dependent on third-party."** Vendor should guarantee they built integration correctly. Third-party system quality is separate issue.

4. **"Maintenance costs are TBD."** Get specific. Usually 10-20% of initial cost annually. Lock it in contract.

5. **"Integration requires customization to your business logic."** That's a red flag. Standard integrations shouldn't require logic customization. Vendor's integration is incomplete.

## FAQ

**Q: Are pre-built integrations always cheaper than custom?**  
A: Yes. Pre-built: ₹0-₹50,000 (already built). Custom: ₹75,000-₹3,00,000 (built for you). Pre-built is always cheaper.

**Q: Can we do integrations ourselves?**  
A: Only if you have developer expertise. Most schools don't have developer on staff. Vendor integration is safer.

**Q: What if vendor says "integration isn't possible"?**  
A: Vendor either lacks integration expertise or the third-party system isn't flexible. Either way, problem. Push for API-first architecture from ERP vendor.

**Q: Should integration be included in ERP pricing or separate?**  
A: Ideally included. Some vendors bundle; others charge separately. Negotiate to include 1-2 essential integrations. Nice-to-have can be separate.

**Q: How long does integration take?**  
A: Pre-built: 1-2 weeks. Custom simple: 3-4 weeks. Custom complex: 8-12 weeks. Plan accordingly.
