## Key Elements Explained

### Learning Objectives (Bloom's Taxonomy)

- **Remember:** Recall facts (list, identify, name)
- **Understand:** Grasp meaning (explain, describe, summarize)
- **Apply:** Use knowledge (solve, demonstrate, apply)
- **Analyze:** Break down (compare, contrast, distinguish)
- **Evaluate:** Make judgments (judge, critique, defend)
- **Create:** Combine to make new (design, invent, construct)

Focus on higher-order thinking (Analyze, Evaluate, Create) for better learning.

### Time Management

Lesson duration: 45 minutes (typical class period)
- Introduction: 5 min (10%)
- Development: 25 min (55%)
- Practice: 10 min (22%)
- Closure: 5 min (11%)

Adjust percentages based on topic and class needs.

### Differentiation

Every class has learners at different levels. Differentiation means:
- **Advanced:** Extension activities, complex problems, leadership roles
- **Struggling:** Simplified explanations, more practice, peer support
- **Special needs:** Modified tasks, extra time, alternate formats

## Best Practices for Lesson Planning

1. **Align to curriculum:** Every lesson should map to specific NCERT/board topic
2. **Clear objectives:** What exactly will students be able to DO?
3. **Variety of methods:** Mix lecture, discussion, practice, projects
4. **Engagement:** Start with hook; maintain interest throughout
5. **Assessment:** How will you know students learned?
6. **Differentiation:** Support all learners
7. **Time management:** Plan realistically; leave buffer time
8. **Reflection:** After teaching, note what worked and what didn't
