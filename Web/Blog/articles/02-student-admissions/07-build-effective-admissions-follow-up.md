## Executive Summary

Effective follow-up converts 40% of inquiries into applications. Poor follow-up loses 70%. This article outlines a systematic follow-up process with templates and cadence.

---

## Follow-Up Cadence

**Day 0 (Inquiry Received):** Auto-SMS confirmation
**Day 1-2:** Information package + personal call
**Day 5:** Application reminder
**Day 10:** Final reminder + deadline warning
**Day 15+:** Mark as lost if no application

---

## Follow-Up Templates

### SMS (Day 0)
"Hi [Family Name], thank you for inquiring about XYZ School. Reference: A12345. Our team will call within 24 hours. Best regards."

### Email (Day 1)
Subject: "Welcome to XYZ School – Your Admissions Package"
Body: Attached fee structure, application form, admissions timeline.

### Call Script (Day 2)
"Hi, this is [Name] from XYZ School. Did you receive our information? Do you have questions? When would you like to apply?"

## FAQ

**Q: How many follow-up touches before we stop?**
A: 3-4 maximum. After that, mark as "low interest."

**Q: Can follow-up be fully automated?**
A: SMS/email yes. But calls must be personal.

**Q: What if families complain about too many follow-ups?**
A: Respect opt-out. But 3-4 touches is standard, not excessive.
