# Admissions Funnel Analytics: Measuring Your Marketing Effectiveness

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## The Funnel: Track Every Step

Website visitors → Inquiries → Campus tours → Applications → Enrollments

Each step has a conversion rate. Where are you leaking?

- 1,000 visitors, but only 50 inquire (5% conversion)
- 50 inquire, but only 20 tour (40% conversion)
- 20 tour, but only 10 apply (50% conversion)
- 10 apply, 8 enroll (80% conversion)

Bottleneck: Inquiry stage. Focus optimization there.

---

## Strategy: Track Your Funnel

### 1. **Define Your Funnel**
- Stage 1: Website visitor
- Stage 2: Inquiry (phone call, form, email)
- Stage 3: Campus tour booked
- Stage 4: Application submitted
- Stage 5: Enrollment confirmed

### 2. **Track Each Stage**
Set up tracking:
- Google Analytics: Website visitors
- Phone tracking: Calls from ads
- Form tracking: Form submissions
- CRM/Admissions: Tours booked, applications, enrollments

### 3. **Calculate Conversion Rates**
- Visitor to inquiry: Inquiries ÷ Visitors
- Inquiry to tour: Tours ÷ Inquiries
- Tour to application: Apps ÷ Tours
- Application to enrollment: Enrollments ÷ Apps
- Overall: Enrollments ÷ Visitors

### 4. **Identify Bottlenecks**
Which stage has lowest conversion?
- Low visitors to inquiry? Fix website conversion
- Low inquiry to tour? Improve follow-up
- Low tour to application? Improve tour experience
- Low app to enrollment? Improve offer/fee

### 5. **Monthly Metrics Dashboard**
Track:
- Visitors: 1,000
- Inquiries: 50 (5% conversion)
- Tours: 20 (40% conversion)
- Applications: 10 (50% conversion)
- Enrollments: 8 (80% conversion)
- Total: 0.8% visitor to enrollment

### 6. **Set Targets**
Target:
- Visitor to inquiry: 2–5%
- Inquiry to tour: 40–60%
- Tour to application: 50–70%
- Application to enrollment: 70–90%
- Overall: 1–2% visitor to enrollment

### 7. **Optimize Weak Stages**
If visitor-to-inquiry is low: Improve website form, add pop-ups, improve messaging
If inquiry-to-tour is low: Faster follow-up, better communication
If tour-to-app is low: Improve campus tour experience, address concerns better
If app-to-enrollment is low: Better offer, fee discussion, overcome objections

### 8. **Tools to Track**
- Google Analytics (free): Website visitors
- Phone tracking: CallRail, CallLoop (paid)
- Hotjar or Fullstory: See how users interact with website
- Nexli: Admissions CRM tracks inquiries, tours, applications
