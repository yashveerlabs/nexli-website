# Display Ads: Banner Advertising for Brand Awareness

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Display Ads: Build Brand Awareness

Display ads are banner ads on education websites, parenting blogs, news sites.

Lower immediate ROI than search ads, but builds brand awareness. Prospects see your ads, remember your brand.

---

## Strategy

### Placements
- Education websites
- Parenting blogs
- Local news sites
- Social media news feed

### Creatives
- Static banner ads (300x250, 728x90)
- Animated ads (catch attention)
- Responsive ads (adapt to space)

### Targeting
- Parenting keywords
- Education interest
- Geographic
- Placements (specific websites)

### Budget
- $20-50/day
- Lower cost per impression than search ads

### Measurement
- Impressions (how many saw ad)
- Click-through rate
- Brand lift (remember ad, increase brand recall)
- Website visits

Display ads build brand awareness. Complement search ads. Together, they create top-of-mind presence.
