# Print Advertising for Schools: Flyers, Brochures, Publications

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Print: Still Effective for Local Reach

Print ads in local publications, flyers, brochures still drive inquiries.

Print ROI lower than digital, but complements digital strategy.

---

## Strategy

### Print Materials
- Flyers (4x6, 5x7)
- Brochures (tri-fold, 8.5x11)
- Local newspaper ads
- Magazine ads (parenting, lifestyle)
- Direct mail (postcard to neighborhood)

### Distribution
- Local shops and cafes
- Libraries
- Community centers
- Medical clinics
- Parent groups

### Content
- School name and tagline
- Key benefit (top-ranked, sports, values)
- Contact info (phone, website, QR code)
- QR code links to admissions form
- Compelling image or student photo

### Measurement
- QR code scans (track via unique code per piece)
- Website visits from print (UTM parameters)
- Phone calls from print ads
- Inquiries from print source

### Budget
- Flyers: $200-500 for 1,000 copies
- Brochures: $300-800 for 500 copies
- Newspaper ad: $500-2,000
- Direct mail: $0.50-1 per piece

### Frequency
- Print new flyers quarterly
- Distribute continuously
- Refresh with new designs seasonally
