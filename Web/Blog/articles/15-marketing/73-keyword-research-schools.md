# Keyword Research for School Marketing: Finding Parent Queries

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Keyword Research: What Parents Search

Understanding parent search queries is foundation of SEO strategy.

Schools ranking for "CBSE schools in [City]" get 20+ inquiries monthly.

---

## Strategy

### Tools
- Google Keyword Planner (free)
- Ubersuggest (paid, $12/month)
- Semrush (paid, expensive)
- AnswerThePublic (free)

### Keyword Types
- Navigation: "Best schools in [City]"
- Informational: "How to choose a school"
- Local: "CBSE schools near me"
- Branded: "School name"

### High-Value Keywords for Schools
- "[City] best schools"
- "[City] CBSE schools"
- "Top schools in [City]"
- "Schools near [neighborhood]"
- "Admissions [City]"
- "[Board] schools [City]"

### Selection Criteria
- High volume (1,000+ searches/month)
- Low competition (easy to rank)
- High intent (parents searching, not random)

### Keyword Mining
- Analyze competitor websites
- Check Google search suggestions
- Look at "People Also Ask"
- Analyze Google Analytics (existing keywords you rank for)

### Keyword Grouping
Organize into themes:
- Admissions keywords
- Academic keywords
- Location keywords
- Program keywords

### Long-Tail Keywords
"How to prepare child for CBSE board exams" (long-tail) easier to rank than "CBSE board exams" (head).

Focus on long-tail keywords initially.

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Use Keyword Planner or Ubersuggest
- [ ] Research 30 keywords
- [ ] Identify 5-10 high-value keywords
- [ ] Group by theme
- [ ] Create content for each theme
- [ ] Track rankings monthly

---

## The Bottom Line

Keyword research reveals what parents search. Target those keywords. Rank. Get traffic.
