# Student Ambassador Program: Peer-to-Peer Marketing

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Student Ambassadors: Your Best Marketing Asset

Current students representing school to prospects is most credible marketing.

Parents trust peers more than school marketing. Student ambassadors move families from interested to enrolled.

---

## Program Structure

### Selection
- Recruit 8-10 students (grades 9-12)
- Criteria: Leadership, communication, passion for school
- Formal selection process

### Training
- School history and mission
- Key programs and achievements
- How to give campus tour
- How to answer questions
- Handling objections

### Roles
- Tour guides (campus tours for prospects)
- Open house ambassadors
- Social media features
- Event participants
- Mentors for younger students

### Incentives
- Certificate of recognition
- Scholarship points
- Public recognition
- Letter of recommendation
- Experience building leadership

### Schedule
- 2-3 hours per month commitment
- Flexible scheduling
- Organized training sessions

### Measurement
- Tours conducted per ambassador
- Conversion rate (tour to application)
- Feedback from prospects
- Ambassador retention
