# Retargeting Ads: Following Up With Interested Families

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Retargeting: Converting the 90% Who Leave

90% of website visitors leave without inquiring. They're interested but not ready.

Retargeting ads follow them across the internet and bring them back.

A parent visits your campus tour page. Leaves. Two days later, they see your ad on Facebook: "Still interested? Here's what parents love about us."

They click back. They apply.

Retargeting converts 5–10% of people who otherwise would've been lost.

---

## Strategy: Retargeting Campaign

### 1. **Set Up Tracking Pixel**
Install Facebook Pixel or Google Ads pixel on your website.

This allows you to track visitors and show them ads later.

### 2. **Define Audiences**
- All website visitors (retarget everyone)
- Admissions page visitors (high-intent, but left)
- Form starters (started form but didn't complete)
- Repeat visitors (visited 3+ times, showed interest)

### 3. **Create Retargeting Ad**
Don't repeat the first ad. Create new ad addressing objections.

**First ad (new visitor):**
"Admissions Open. Top-ranked school in [City]. Apply now."

**Retargeting ad (day 2, after they left):**
"Still considering? Here's why 1,000+ parents chose us. [Testimonial quote]. Book a campus tour today."

**Retargeting ad (day 7):**
"Only 5 seats left for Class 6. Limited time. Apply now."

### 4. **Platform Preference**
- Facebook/Instagram: Best for retargeting parents
- Google Ads: Good secondary channel

### 5. **Budget**
Retargeting typically cheaper than new-audience ads.
- Budget: $300–500/month
- Generate 5–15 additional inquiries

### 6. **Duration**
Retarget people for 30–60 days after they leave your site.

After 60 days, they're no longer interested.

### 7. **Creative Testing**
Test different ads:
- Testimonial vs. achievement
- Discount offer vs. limited seats
- Video vs. image

Measure which converts better.

### 8. **Measurement**
Track:
- Clicks from retargeting ads
- Cost per click
- Conversion rate (clicks → inquiries)
- Revenue impact (inquiries from retargeting that became enrollments)

---

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] **Install tracking pixel:** Facebook or Google
- [ ] **Create retargeting audience:** Website visitors
- [ ] **Create 3 retargeting ads:** Different messages
- [ ] **Set budget:** $300–500/month
- [ ] **Launch campaign:** 30-day duration
- [ ] **Monitor:** Cost per click, conversions
- [ ] **Optimize:** Pause low-performers, increase budget on winners

---

## The Bottom Line

Retargeting is your highest-ROI paid channel. Follow up with interested people who left. Convert them.
