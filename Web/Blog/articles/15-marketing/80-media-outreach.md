# Media Outreach: Building Journalist Relationships

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Media Relations: Strategic Journalist Engagement

Journalists need stories. Schools need media coverage. Help them help you.

Build relationships with education journalists. Get regular press coverage.

---

## Strategy

### Find Journalists
- Local education reporters
- School/education beat reporters
- Community journalists
- Parenting bloggers

### Build Relationships
- Email (personalized, not generic)
- Phone call (after email)
- Offer story ideas
- Make interviews easy
- Provide good photos/quotes

### Pitch Ideas
- Student achievements
- New programs
- Awards/recognition
- Research/data
- Human interest stories
- Community impact

### Make Their Job Easy
- Provide ready-made quotes
- Offer professional photos
- Prepare for interviews
- Respond quickly to questions
- Follow up professionally

### Frequency
- 1-2 pitches per term
- Never spam with irrelevant ideas
- Personalize each pitch

### Follow-Up
- After coverage, thank them
- Share their article
- Invite them to events
- Build ongoing relationship

### Measurement
- Media mentions per year
- Reach of articles
- Inquiries from coverage
- Brand awareness

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Build journalist contact list
- [ ] Pitch first story
- [ ] Follow up
- [ ] Track coverage
- [ ] Build relationships long-term

---

## The Bottom Line

Good media relationships = regular press coverage = credibility = more inquiries.
