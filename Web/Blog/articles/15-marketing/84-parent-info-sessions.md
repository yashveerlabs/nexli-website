# Parent Information Sessions: Group Engagement and Lead Generation

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Parent Info Sessions: Group Marketing

Small group presentations for interested parents. Efficient way to engage 20-30 families at once.

20 families in one session = 20 leads captured.

---

## Strategy

### Topics
- "Choosing the Right School: Guide for Parents"
- "How to Support Your Child's Learning"
- "Board Exam Preparation: Parent's Role"
- "Admissions Process Explained"
- "Our Programs and Why They Matter"

### Frequency
- Monthly or quarterly
- Same presentation (repeatable)
- Different times (morning, evening)

### Format
- 45-60 min presentation
- 15 min Q&A
- Followed by campus tour (optional)
- Light refreshments

### Promotion
- Email campaigns
- Social media
- Website banner
- Local WhatsApp groups
- Flyers

### Registration
- Google Form or online
- Capture: Name, email, phone, child's grade
- Confirmation email

### Content
- School overview (10 min)
- Key programs (20 min)
- Student success stories (10 min)
- Q&A (15 min)

### Follow-Up
- Email within 24 hours
- Offer campus tour
- Link to admissions form

### Measurement
- Attendance
- Leads captured
- Tours booked
- Applications submitted
