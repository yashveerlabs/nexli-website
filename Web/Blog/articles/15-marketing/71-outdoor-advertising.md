# Outdoor Advertising: Billboards, Transit Ads, Community Visibility

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Outdoor Ads: Brand Visibility

Billboards, transit ads (buses, auto), hoarding in high-traffic areas.

Build brand recognition through repeated exposure.

---

## Strategy

### Ad Types
- Billboards (large, high-traffic locations)
- Transit ads (buses, train stations)
- Hoardings (construction sites, highways)
- Vehicle wraps (school bus or delivery vehicle)

### Locations
- Near school (highway, main roads)
- Neighborhoods with target audience
- Near competitor schools
- High-traffic areas (malls, marketplaces)

### Messaging
- School name and tagline
- Key benefit
- Contact (phone, website)
- Memorable visual (student, campus, logo)

### Duration
- Minimum 3 months (brand recall builds slowly)
- Typically 6-12 month contracts

### Cost
- Billboard: ₹20,000-50,000/month
- Transit ad: ₹10,000-25,000/month
- Vehicle wrap: ₹2,000-5,000/month

### ROI
Lower immediate ROI than digital, but builds brand awareness over time.

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Identify high-traffic locations
- [ ] Contact outdoor ad providers
- [ ] Design creative
- [ ] Contract for 3-6 months
- [ ] Launch
- [ ] Monitor inquiries/calls mentioning ad
- [ ] Renew if ROI positive

---

## The Bottom Line

Outdoor ads build long-term brand awareness. Complements digital strategy. Essential for local visibility.
