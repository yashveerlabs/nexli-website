# Admissions Funnel Email Sequence: Converting Prospects to Applicants

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Admissions Email Sequence: Guiding Prospects

A prospect fills your admissions form. Then what? Email sequence keeps momentum.

Day 1: Confirmation + admissions process
Day 3: Campus tour booking link
Day 7: Fee structure and programs
Day 14: Application deadline reminder
Day 21: Final push + limited seats message

Each email moves them toward enrollment.

---

## Strategy

### 1. **Email 1: Confirmation**
- Confirm receipt
- Thank them
- Next steps (tour booking link)
- Expected response time

### 2. **Email 2: Campus Tour**
- Invite to campus tour
- Show tour benefits
- Booking link/button
- Multiple dates available

### 3. **Email 3: Programs**
- Explain programs/curriculum
- Fee structure overview
- Scholarship availability
- Student testimonial

### 4. **Email 4: Deadline**
- Admissions deadline reminder
- Urgency ("Only 5 seats left")
- Quick application link
- Questions? Contact us

### 5. **Email 5: Final**
- "Don't miss out"
- Limited seats message
- Enroll now button
- Personal touch (principal message)

### 2. **Timing**
Send over 2-3 weeks.
Don't overwhelm (1 email every 3-4 days).

### 3. **Personalization**
- Use prospect name
- Reference child's grade
- Mention their stated interest

### 4. **CTAs**
Each email has 1 clear CTA:
- Book tour
- View programs
- Apply now
- Call us

### 5. **Segmentation**
Different sequences for:
- Different grades
- Different program interests
- Different inquiry sources
