# Annual Marketing Plan: Building Yearly Strategy and Timeline

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Annual Plan: Strategic Marketing Blueprint

A written annual plan ensures consistent, strategic execution. Unplanned marketing is random and ineffective.

Schools with annual plans grow 2x faster than schools without.

---

## Plan Components

### Goals
- Admissions target (new enrollments/year)
- Revenue target
- Brand awareness metrics
- Website traffic targets

### Budget
- Total marketing budget
- Allocation by channel (Google Ads, Facebook, events, etc.)
- Monthly budget cadence
- Contingency fund (10%)

### Quarterly Themes
- Q1: Admissions push (January-March)
- Q2: Open houses (April-June)
- Q3: Planning and awareness (July-September)
- Q4: Final push (October-December)

### Campaigns
- Major campaigns per quarter
- Timelines and responsible owners
- Budget per campaign
- Target metrics

### Content Calendar
- Blog publishing schedule
- Social media content calendar
- Email newsletters
- Video production schedule
- Webinar schedule

### Channel Strategy
- Website (SEO, blog, conversions)
- Paid ads (Google, Facebook budgets)
- Social media (content, engagement)
- Email (newsletters, automation)
- PR and media (press releases, pitches)
- Events (open houses, sponsorships)

### Team and Roles
- Who owns what
- Responsibilities
- Timeline for execution

### Measurement
- How to track each initiative
- Reporting cadence
- Review process
- Optimization triggers

### Contingency
- What if enrollment is down?
- What if budget is cut?
- Plan B channels
