- Build basic website
- Set up Google Business Profile

- Launch social media

### Layer 2: Efficiency (Months 4-6)
- Optimize website conversion
- Launch Google Ads
- Create blog content
- Build email automation
- Establish crisis protocols
- Train admissions team

**Metrics:** 300 visitors/month, 50 inquiries/month, 12 enrollments/month

### Layer 3: Scale (Months 7-12)
- Optimize each channel (what's working, kill what's not)
- Launch Facebook/Instagram ads
- Build referral program
- Establish partnerships
- Get PR coverage
- Create video content

**Metrics:** 600 visitors/month, 100 inquiries/month, 25 enrollments/month

### Layer 4: Dominance (Year 2+)
- Market leader in your city
- Multiple successful channels
- Strong referral engine

- Enrollment exceeds capacity

**Metrics:** 1,000+ visitors/month, 150+ inquiries/month, 40+ enrollments/month

## The 5 Pillars of Mastery

### 1. Brand
- Clear positioning (who you are, who you serve)
- Consistent messaging
- Visible leadership
- Community respect
- Reputation for excellence

### 2. Website
- Mobile-first, fast loading
- Optimized for conversions
- SEO-optimized
- Compelling copy
- Clear CTAs

### 3. Content
- Blog posts (SEO, lead gen, thought leadership)
- Social content (engagement, virality)
- Video (awareness, education)
- Email (nurturing, retention)
- PR (authority, credibility)

### 4. Channels
- Owned (website, email, blog, app)
- Paid (ads, sponsorships, PR)
- Earned (media, reviews, referrals)
- Social (organic + paid)
- Community (events, partnerships)

### 5. Systems
- Marketing dashboard (measurement)
- Annual plan (strategy)
- Content calendar (consistency)
- Email automation (scale)
- Analytics (optimization)
