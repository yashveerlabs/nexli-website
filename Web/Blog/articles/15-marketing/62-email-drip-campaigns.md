# Email Drip Campaigns: Nurturing Leads Automatically

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Drip Campaigns: Automated Nurturing

A prospect downloads your lead magnet. Automatically, they receive 5 emails over 20 days. Each moves them closer to enrollment.

Drip campaigns convert 15–25% automatically, without sales team effort.

---

## Strategy

### 1. **Sequence Design**
Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + deliver lead magnet
Email 2 (Day 3): "Here's what parents love about us"
Email 3 (Day 7): Student transformation story
Email 4 (Day 14): "Ready to enroll? Process explained"
Email 5 (Day 20): Final CTA ("Book a tour")

### 2. **Personalization**
- Use prospect name
- Reference their specific interest
- Address their stated concerns

### 3. **Content Mix**
- 40% Educational value
- 30% Testimonials/proof
- 30% CTA/sales

### 4. **Timing**
- Send every 3–4 days (not too frequent)
- Monday–Thursday best days
- 9–10 AM or 6–7 PM best times

### 5. **Segmentation**
Different sequences for:
- New inquiries
- Website visitors (no form)
- Open house attendees
- Form-starters (didn't complete)

### 6. **Tools**
- Mailchimp (free)
- Brevo
- Nexli (built-in)
- HubSpot (advanced)

### 7. **Measurement**
- Open rate by email
- Click rate
- Conversion rate (emails → inquiries)
- Which emails drive actions
