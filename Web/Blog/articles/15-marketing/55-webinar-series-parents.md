# Webinar Series for Parents: Building Community Online

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Webinars: Interactive Parent Engagement

Live webinars build community and capture leads. Parents register (email capture), attend live, ask questions, build connection.

Webinar conversion rate: 30–50% of attendees become inquiries.

---

## Webinar Strategy

### 1. **Topics**
- How to support your child's learning
- Board exam preparation for parents
- College admissions explained
- Career counseling 101
- Parenting teens effectively
- Growth mindset for parents
- Learning styles and your child
- Digital citizenship and screen time

### 2. **Format**
- 45–60 minutes
- Hosted online (Zoom, Google Meet)
- Expert speaker (principal, counselor, external guest)
- Q&A portion
- Recorded for later viewing

### 3. **Promotion**
- Email (2 weeks before)
- Social media (weekly reminders)
- Website banner
- Parent WhatsApp groups
- Flyers

### 4. **Lead Capture**
Registration form captures:
- Name, email, phone
- Child's grade
- Interest in specific programs

### 5. **Frequency**
1 webinar per quarter = 4 per year

### 6. **Follow-Up**
- Email recording to attendees (day 1)
- Follow-up email: "Questions? Book a campus tour" (day 3)
- Add to mailing list for future emails

### 7. **Repurpose**
- YouTube upload
- Blog post (transcription)
- Social media clips
- Podcast episode
- Email mini-course
