# Voice Search Optimization: Preparing for Future SEO

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Voice Search: The Future

30% of searches are now voice (Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Siri).

Voice searches are conversational ("What are good schools near me?") vs. typed searches ("best schools near me").

Optimizing for voice means capturing this growing segment.

---

## Strategy

### Conversational Keywords
Traditional: "best schools [city]"
Voice: "What are the best schools near me?"

Optimize for question-based keywords:
- "Which schools are near me?"
- "What schools offer sports?"
- "How much does [school] charge?"

### FAQ Content
- Create FAQ page
- Answer common voice queries
- Use question + answer format
- Natural language

### Local Optimization
- Google My Business complete (voice relies on it)
- Local citations
- Local keywords
- Phone number prominent

### Page Structure
- Clear headings (H2 for questions)
- Short paragraphs
- Direct answers
- Featured snippets (Google pulls voice results from these)

### Mobile Optimization
- 90% voice searches on mobile
- Mobile-first design essential
- Fast loading

### Measurement
- Track voice search traffic (if possible)
- Monitor featured snippet appearances
- Track question-based keywords
- Monitor "People Also Ask" features
