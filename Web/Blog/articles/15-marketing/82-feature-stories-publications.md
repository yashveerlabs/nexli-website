# Feature Stories in Publications: Getting Deeper Media Coverage

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Feature Stories: Deep Dives That Build Reputation

A feature story is a long-form (1,500+ word) article about your school, principal, or program.

Feature stories reach 100,000+ readers. Massive awareness boost.

---

## Strategy

### Feature Topics
- Principal's leadership journey
- Unique program or approach
- Student success story
- Community impact story
- Innovation/research
- Teacher excellence

### Pitching Publications
- Education magazines
- Local business journals
- Parenting magazines
- National education publications
- Online education blogs

### Pitch Process
- Research publication
- Personalized pitch email
- Compelling story angle
- Offer access/interviews
- Provide professional photos

### Collaboration
- Work with journalist
- Provide good quotes
- Share necessary documents
- Proofread before publication
- Share widely after publication

### Measurement
- Reach of article (publication circulation)
- Inquiries from feature
- Social media shares
- Brand lift
