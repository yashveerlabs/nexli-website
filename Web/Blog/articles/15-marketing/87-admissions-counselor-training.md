# Admissions Counselor Training: Building Staff Capability

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Admissions Team: Your Marketing Force

Your admissions team is your frontline marketing. Poor training = lost inquiries.

Well-trained admissions staff convert 40-50% of inquiries. Untrained staff convert 5-10%.

---

## Training Topics

### Product Knowledge
- School programs and curriculum
- Unique differentiators
- Fee structure
- Admission process
- Key statistics (rankings, achievements)

### Sales Skills
- How to listen
- How to qualify leads
- How to handle objections
- How to close (convert to campus tour)
- Follow-up procedures

### Customer Service
- Professionalism
- Responsiveness (within 2 hours)
- Problem-solving
- Building rapport
- Managing difficult conversations

### Technology
- CRM system
- Email platforms
- Scheduling tools
- Website forms
- Tracking metrics

### Communication
- Email writing
- Phone etiquette
- WhatsApp communication
- Presentation skills
- Parent objection handling

### Strategy

- Initial training (full day, role-play)
- Monthly refresher sessions
- Performance metrics
- Incentives for conversion
- Feedback and coaching

### Measurement
- Inquiry response time
- Conversion rate (inquiry to tour)
- Tour to application rate
- Customer satisfaction

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Document admissions process
- [ ] Create training manual
- [ ] Conduct initial training
- [ ] Monthly refreshers
- [ ] Track performance metrics
- [ ] Coaching and feedback

---

## The Bottom Line

Well-trained admissions staff is your best marketing investment. Training pays for itself in higher conversion rates.
