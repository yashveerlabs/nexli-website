# School Scholarship Publicity: Attracting Merit and Need-Based Students

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Scholarships: Marketing Tool and Social Impact

Scholarships aren't just aid. They're marketing.

Publicize scholarships prominently. Feature recipients. Tell their stories. This attracts quality students and builds reputation for inclusivity and opportunity.

**The data:** Schools highlighting scholarship programs see 15–20% more diversity inquiries and 10% more total applications.

---

## Strategy: Scholarship-Driven Marketing

### 1. **Publicize Scholarship Availability**
Feature prominently:
- Website homepage: "Scholarships available for meritorious students"
- Social media: Monthly "Scholarship Spotlight"
- Email: Highlight in admissions newsletter
- Ads: Google/Facebook ads targeting "school scholarships"

### 2. **Feature Scholarship Recipients**
Tell their stories:
- "Meet Arjun: Merit Scholar. 95% in board exams. Now IIT student."
- "Meet Priya: Need-based Scholar. Overcame challenges. Now class topper."

### 3. **Highlight Selection Criteria**
Be transparent:
- Merit scholarships: Top grades, entrance exam performance
- Need-based: Income, family circumstances
- Sports scholarships: Achievements, potential
- Special talent: Arts, music, coding

### 4. **Application Process Visibility**
Make it easy to apply:
- Clear guidelines
- Simple application form
- Timeline visible
- Support available

### 5. **Annual Celebration**
Host scholarship awards ceremony:
- Recognize all recipients
- Share stories
- Photo/video coverage
- Share on social media

### 6. **Alumni Engagement**
Use alumni for fundraising:
- "Successful alumnus endows scholarship in memory of [Teacher]"
- Feature alumni donors in newsletter

---

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] **Define scholarship types:** Merit, need-based, sports, talent
- [ ] **Set amounts and criteria:** Clear eligibility
- [ ] **Publicize availability:** Website, social, ads
- [ ] **Feature recipients:** Story, photo, impact
- [ ] **Create application process:** Simple, transparent
- [ ] **Celebrate annually:** Awards ceremony with media coverage
- [ ] **Track impact:** Measure diversity improvements

---

## The Bottom Line

Scholarships are your proof that you care about opportunity. Publicize them widely. Build reputation for inclusivity and access.
