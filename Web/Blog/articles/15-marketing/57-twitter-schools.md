# Twitter for Schools: News and Real-Time Updates

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Twitter: Real-Time School Updates

Use Twitter for:
- Breaking news (school closures, events)
- Live event updates (annual day, sports)
- Thoughts on education
- Quick tips and facts
- Engagement with education community

Twitter reach is lower for schools than Instagram/Facebook, but highly engaged audience.

---

## Strategy

### 1. **Content Types**
- Event updates
- Achievement announcements
- Educational insights
- Retweets of education news
- Community engagement
- Parent tips

### 2. **Posting**
- 1–2 tweets per day (or as news breaks)
- Use hashtags (#Education, #Schools, #[City]Schools)
- Engage with education conversations
- Reply to mentions

### 3. **Followers**
- Tag parents, educators, education journalists
- Follow education accounts
- Engage with relevant tweets
- Build community

### 4. **Links**
- Link to blog posts
- Link to YouTube videos
- Link to admissions form

### 5. **Hashtags**
Use 2–4 hashtags per tweet:
- #Education
- #[School Name]
- #[City]Schools
- #IndianEducation

## The Bottom Line

Twitter is secondary channel for schools. Good for real-time updates and educator engagement. Not primary lead source but important for brand presence.
