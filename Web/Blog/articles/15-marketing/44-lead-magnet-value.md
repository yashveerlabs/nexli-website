# Lead Magnet: Offering Value to Capture Parent Contact

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Lead Magnet: Free Value in Exchange for Contact

A parent visits your website. They're not ready to apply yet. So they leave. You lose them.

A lead magnet captures their contact before they leave.

"Download: Parent's Guide to Choosing a School" (in exchange for email).

Now you have their contact. You nurture them via email. 6 months later, they're ready to enroll.

---

## Strategy: Create Lead Magnets

### 1. **Lead Magnet Ideas**
- "Parent's Guide to Choosing a School" (PDF)
- "CBSE Board Exam Checklist" (PDF)
- "50 Interview Questions Your Child Will Face" (PDF)
- "School Transition Guide for New Parents" (PDF)
- Webinar: "How to Support Your Child's Learning" (email signup to attend)
- Video: "Campus tour" (email to access)
- Checklist: "What to Look for in a School" (PDF)

### 2. **Make It Valuable**
The lead magnet must be genuinely useful, not promotional.

Good: "5 ways to reduce exam anxiety in children" (actionable advice)
Bad: "Our school's brochure" (promotional, not useful)

### 3. **Format**
Best formats for conversion:
- PDF guide (easy to download, read offline)
- Checklist (actionable, printable)
- Webinar (interactive, personal)
- Video (engaging, memorable)

### 4. **Distribution**
Promote lead magnet through:
- Website (pop-up, banner, dedicated landing page)
- Social media (link in bio, pinned post)
- Ads (Google, Facebook)
- Email (to current parents, alumni)
- In-person (QR code at open house, events)

### 5. **Capture Form**
Form should ask:
- Full name
- Email
- Phone
- Child's grade/age

Don't ask too much. People won't give data for little value.

### 6. **Follow-Up Sequence**
Once captured:
- Email 1 (same day): "Download your guide"
- Email 2 (day 3): Related tip
- Email 3 (day 7): Parent testimonial
- Email 4 (day 14): Admissions info
- Email 5+ (ongoing): Nurturing emails

Nurture sequence converts prospects at 10–20% rate over 3 months.

### 7. **Measure ROI**
Track:
- Lead magnets downloaded: X
- Leads converted to inquiries: Y
- Inquiries converted to enrollments: Z
- Cost per download: Cost ÷ Downloads
- Cost per enrollment: Cost ÷ Enrollments
