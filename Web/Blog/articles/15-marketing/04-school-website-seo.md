## School Website SEO: Getting Found Online

**When parents search "schools near me" or "best CBSE schools in [city]", can they find you? SEO (search engine optimization) makes your website visible in Google. This guide covers keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, and local SEO.**

---

## Keyword Research: What Are Parents Searching?

**High-Intent Keywords (searching to enroll):**
- "Schools in [City]"
- "[City] CBSE schools"
- "Best schools in [District]"
- "Residential schools near [City]"
- "Schools with sports focus"
- "Affordable schools [City]"

**Informational Keywords (research):**
- "How to choose a school"
- "NEP 2020 Holistic Progress Card"
- "International Baccalaureate vs. CBSE"
- "Boarding school benefits"

**Strategy:** Target 20–30 keywords relevant to your school. Use Google Keyword Planner (free) to check search volume.

---

## On-Page SEO

**Title Tags (appears in Google results):**
- "Best CBSE School in [City] | Admissions Open"
- Include location and keyword (improves ranking)

**Meta Descriptions (snippet under title):**
- "Award-winning CBSE school with 85% pass rate. Holistic education. Admissions open for 2026–27."
- 155 characters max (longer cuts off)

**Headings & Content:**
- Use target keyword in H1 (main title), H2 (sections)
- Write for humans, not just search engines (Google penalizes keyword stuffing)
- 500+ words per page (longer content ranks better)
- Internal links (link to your other pages: "Read about our academics" → Academics page)

**Images:**
- Optimize file names: "school-science-lab.jpg" not "IMG_12345.jpg"
- Add alt text: "Group of students conducting chemistry experiment in modern lab"

---

## Technical SEO

**Site Speed:**
- Test with Google PageSpeed Insights
- Goal: <3 second load time
- Compress images, minimize code, use CDN

**Mobile Responsiveness:**
- Design looks good on mobile (where 60% of searches happen)
- Test with Google Mobile-Friendly Test

**Sitemap & Robots.txt:**
- XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
- Robots.txt allows Google to crawl your site

**SSL Certificate:**
- Website must be HTTPS (secure)
- Google penalizes non-HTTPS sites

---

## Local SEO (Most Important for Schools)

**Google Business Profile:**
1. Create free business listing on Google
2. Add school name, address, phone, hours
3. Add school description
4. Post updates and events
5. Upload photos

**Local Citations:**
- List school in directories (Justdial, Facebook, local business listings)
- Consistent name, address, phone across all listings
- Improves local ranking

**Reviews:**
- Encourage parents to leave Google reviews (positive reviews boost ranking)
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- 4+ star average = higher ranking

---

## Backlinks (Links From Other Sites)

**Get links from high-authority sites:**
- Local news coverage ("School Wins Science Olympiad")
- Education directories
- Alumni/parent websites

**Internal linking:**
- Link between your pages (academics page → Admissions page)
- Improves crawlability and ranking

---

## Content Strategy for SEO

**Create content around keywords:**
- Blog post: "How to Choose the Right School: 10 Factors Parents Should Consider"
- Blog post: "NEP 2020 Explained: What It Means for Your Child's Education"
- Blog post: "Best CBSE Schools in [City]: What to Look For"

**Update regularly:**
- Fresh content signals to Google that your site is active
- Publish 1 blog post per month minimum

**Link to external authority:**
- Reference credible sources (MHRD, CBSE, Vedantu)
- Shows Google you're trustworthy

---

## Measure & Monitor

**Google Search Console (free):**
- See which keywords bring traffic
- Identify pages with low clicks (improve them)
- Monitor indexing issues

**Google Analytics (free):**
- Track visits, bounce rate, time on page
- See which pages convert (lead inquiries)

**Goal:** +50% organic traffic in 6 months, +100% in 12 months

## FAQ

**Q1: How long for SEO results?**  
A: 3–6 months to see ranking improvements. Patience required.

**Q2: Should we use an SEO agency?**  
A: If budget allows (₹3K–10K/month), yes. Otherwise, follow this guide and optimize yourself.

**Q3: Is paid Google Ads better than SEO?**  
A: Ads are faster (immediate), SEO is slower (3–6 months) but free long-term. Do both.
