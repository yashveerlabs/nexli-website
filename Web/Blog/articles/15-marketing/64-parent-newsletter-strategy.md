# Parent Newsletter: Keeping Families Engaged Monthly

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Parent Newsletter: Ongoing Engagement

Monthly newsletters keep parents informed, connected, and engaged.

Schools with monthly newsletters retain parents 20% longer.

---

## Strategy

### Content Mix
- 30% Student achievements
- 25% Upcoming events
- 20% Parent tips/advice
- 15% Community stories
- 10% CTA/admissions

### Design
- Branded template (consistent colors, fonts)
- Mobile-responsive
- Clear hierarchy (big headlines, scannable)
- 1-2 images per section

### Topics by Month
- June: Welcome to new year
- July-Aug: Summer programs
- Sept-Oct: Academic excellence
- Nov-Dec: Celebrations and giving
- Jan-Feb: New resolutions
- Mar-Apr: College admissions

### Frequency
Once per month (consistent date, e.g., first Monday)

### Lead Gen
Include CTA for admissions/tours in every newsletter.

### Measurement
- Open rate (40%+ target)
- Click rate (5%+ target)
- Shares
- Unsubscribe rate (<0.5% target)
