## Google Ads for School Admissions: Paid Marketing Strategy

**Google Ads puts your school in front of parents searching for schools. Unlike SEO (3–6 month wait), Google Ads drives immediate traffic. This guide covers campaign setup, keyword selection, budget, and ROI.**

---

## How Google Ads Works

**Parent searches:** "Best schools in [City]" or "CBSE schools near me"
**Your ad appears:** Top of Google results (marked as "Ad")
**You pay:** Only when parent clicks (Pay-Per-Click or PPC)
**Parent lands:** On your website or landing page
**Conversion:** Parent fills inquiry form, becomes a lead

---

## Campaign Setup

**Step 1: Google Ads Account**
- Create account at google.com/ads
- Link to website and Google Analytics
- Setup billing

**Step 2: Keywords**
- High-intent keywords: "[City] schools", "best CBSE schools [City]"
- Long-tail: "affordable schools in [City]", "schools with good academics"
- Avoid: Too broad ("schools") = wasted spend

**Step 3: Ad Copy**
- Headline: "Best CBSE School in [City] | Admissions Open"
- Description: "85% pass rate, modern facilities, enroll today"
- CTA: "Apply Now", "Request Info"

**Step 4: Landing Page**
- Where does the ad click go? Should be admissions page or dedicated landing page
- Remove navigation (focus on conversion)
- Single CTA ("Apply Now" button)

**Step 5: Budget**
- Daily budget: ₹500–2,000/day (adjust based on leads needed)
- Campaign starts immediately

---

## Bid Strategy

**Pay-Per-Click (PPC):** You set max bid per keyword
- High-intent keyword: ₹50–100 per click
- Medium-intent keyword: ₹20–50 per click
- Low-intent keyword: ₹5–20 per click

**Example:** If you get 10 clicks/day at ₹50/click = ₹500/day = ₹15K/month budget

---

## Measuring ROI

**Key metrics:**
- Cost Per Click (CPC): How much per click? (₹50 avg)
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): % of impressions that click (target: 5%+)
- Conversion Rate: % of clicks that become leads (target: 10%+)
- Cost Per Lead: ₹500/click × 10% conversion = ₹5,000/lead

**Profitable if:**
- Cost per lead <5% of annual fee
- 1 lead converts to 1 admission
- Example: ₹5,000/lead, ₹1L annual fee = 5% CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = profitable

---

## Campaign Optimization

**Month 1–2:** Collect data (100+ clicks minimum)
**Month 3:** Pause underperforming keywords, boost winners
**Ongoing:** A/B test ad copy, landing pages, keywords

**Budget allocation:**
- 70% on high-performing keywords
- 20% on medium performers (for growth)
- 10% on experimental (new keywords, testing)

---

## Common Mistakes

**Bidding on brand name only:** Miss "schools in [City]" searches
**Poor landing page:** Slow, not mobile-friendly, no clear CTA → high bounce
**No tracking:** Can't measure ROI → waste money blindly
**Broad keywords:** "Education" gets clicks from teacher job searches → wasted spend
