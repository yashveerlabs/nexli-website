# Backlinks Strategy: Building Domain Authority for Schools

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Backlinks: The Currency of Authority

A backlink is when another website links to yours.

Google treats backlinks as "votes" for your authority. More backlinks = higher authority = better rankings.

Schools with 20+ quality backlinks rank #1 for target keywords.

---

## Strategy

### Types of Backlinks
- **High-quality:** Education blogs, news sites, official directories
- **Medium:** Local business directories, school lists
- **Low:** Link farms, spam sites (avoid)

### How to Build Backlinks

1. **Create Valuable Content**
- Write research articles schools link to
- Create infographics (shareable, link-worthy)
- Create guides or tools

2. **Guest Blogging**
- Write article for education blog
- Include link back to your site
- Build relationship

3. **Get Mentioned in News**
- Award submissions
- Press releases
- Media features
- News coverage

4. **Partnerships**
- Partner with local businesses
- Mutual linking
- Directory submissions

5. **Broken Link Building**
- Find broken links on relevant sites
- Offer your content as replacement
- Get backlink

### Where to Get Backlinks
- Education blogs
- Parenting websites
- Local news sites
- Directory listings
- Industry databases
- Alumni networks
- Partner organizations

### Quality Over Quantity
- 5 links from authoritative sites > 50 links from spam sites
- Focus on domain authority (DA 30+)
- Relevance matters (education-related sites link to schools)

### Measurement
- Use Ubersuggest or Semrush
- Track backlink count
- Monitor referring domains
- Check domain authority
