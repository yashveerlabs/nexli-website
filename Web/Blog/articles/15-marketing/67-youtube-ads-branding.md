# YouTube Ads for School Brand Building

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## YouTube Ads: Reach at Scale

YouTube has 2 billion monthly users. Parents watch education videos, parenting content, tips.

YouTube Ads reach them with brand-building video content.

---

## Strategy

### Ad Types
- In-stream ads (skippable after 5 sec, play before video)
- Discovery ads (appear in search results)
- Bumper ads (6-sec non-skippable brand ads)

### Creatives
- Campus tour (full 3-5 min)
- Student testimonial (2 min)
- Parent testimonial (2 min)
- Educational tips with branding (3 min)
- Event highlights (1-2 min)

### Targeting
- Parents ages 25-55
- Parenting, education interests
- Geographic (your city)
- Similar to website visitors (retargeting)

### Bidding
- Cost per view: $0.25-1.50
- Set daily budget: $20-50/day

### Measurement
- Views (goal: high view count)
- Click-through rate to website
- Cost per view
- Conversion rate (views → inquiries)

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Create YouTube Ads account

- [ ] Define targeting
- [ ] Set budget: $20-50/day
- [ ] Launch campaign
- [ ] Monitor views and CPC
- [ ] Optimize targeting
- [ ] Scale based on ROI

YouTube reaches mass audience with video. Good for brand awareness and reaching new families.
