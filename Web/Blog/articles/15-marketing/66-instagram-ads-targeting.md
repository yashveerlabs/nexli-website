# Instagram Ads: Targeting Parents on Visual Platform

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Instagram Ads: Visual, Engaging, Effective

Instagram Ads reach parents where they spend time. Visual platform with high engagement.

Instagram Ads ROI: 3-4x (generate 8-15 inquiries per $1,000 spent).

---

## Strategy

### Targeting
- Parents ages 25-55
- Location: Your city + 30km radius
- Interests: Education, parenting, schools
- Behaviors: School-age children

### Creatives
- Student success photos (inspiring)
- Campus beauty shots
- Testimonial videos
- Event highlights
- Enrollment CTAs

### Ad Formats
- Photo ads (single image + text)
- Video ads (15-60 sec)
- Carousel (multiple images, swipeable)
- Stories ads (vertical, 9:16)
- Reels ads (short videos)

### Budget & ROI
- $20/day ($600/month) = 8-15 inquiries
- Cost per inquiry: $40-75
- Scale winners, pause underperformers

### Measurement
- Click-through rate (3-5% healthy)
- Cost per click ($1-3)
- Conversion rate (clicks to inquiries)
- Cost per inquiry ($40-75 target)
