# Video Content for Schools: Strategy and Production

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Video: The Most Engaging Content Format

Video marketing has 80% higher engagement than text. Parents watch videos 3x more than they read articles.

Schools with active video marketing get 5x more inquiries.

---

## Video Strategy

### 1. **Video Types**
- Campus tour (10 min)
- Student testimonial (2 min)
- Day-in-life (5 min)
- Teacher interview (5 min)
- Event highlights (3 min)
- Educational tips (3 min)
- Parent testimonial (2 min)
- Alumni success (3 min)

### 2. **Production**
- Phone camera (good enough)
- Natural lighting
- Vertical format for mobile
- Captions (60% watch muted)
- Music (royalty-free)

### 3. **Distribution**
- YouTube (main channel)
- Instagram Reels (short clips)
- Facebook (native upload)
- Website (embedded)
- Email (link)
- Ads (promotional)

### 4. **Frequency**
- 2–4 videos per month (sustainable)
- Mix of lengths (short + long)
- Consistent upload day/time

### 5. **Measurement**
- Views
- Watch time
- Engagement (likes, comments)
- Conversions (clicks to website)
- Lead generation
