# Local Search Ads: "School Near Me" Optimization

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Local Ads: Capture "Near Me" Searches

When parent searches "schools near me" on Google Maps, they see ads from local schools.

Local ads drive immediate action (calls, map visits, website clicks).

---

## Strategy

### Ad Types
- Local services ads (Google Maps)
- Local search ads (Google Search)
- Local campaigns (automated ad creation)

### Targeting
- Geographic (your city, neighborhoods)
- Local keywords ("schools near [neighborhood]")
- High-intent queries ("admissions [city]")

### Messaging
- School name
- Location and hours
- Main benefit ("Top-ranked CBSE school")
- CTA ("Call," "Get directions," "Visit website")

### Budget
- $20-50/day
- Cost per lead: $30-60

### Measurement
- Phone calls from ads
- Map directions clicked
- Website visits
- Website conversions
