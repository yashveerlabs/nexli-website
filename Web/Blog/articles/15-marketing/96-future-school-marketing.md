## Future Trends: What's Coming in School Marketing

Technology, AI, and new platforms reshape school marketing. Stay ahead.

Early adopters of new channels get 3x more market share.

---

## Trends to Watch

### AI & Personalization
- AI chatbots answering parent questions
- Personalized email sequences
- Predictive analytics (who will enroll)
- AI-generated content recommendations

### Video Everything
- Live streaming school events
- Virtual campus tours (VR/360)
- Student video portfolios
- AI-generated video summaries

### Voice Search
- "Best schools near me" queries
- Smart speaker school info
- Voice-activated forms
- Conversational AI

### Social Commerce
- Shop directly from Instagram/TikTok
- One-click enrollment
- Virtual try-ons/tours

### Metaverse
- Virtual school campus tours
- Metaverse school communities
- Digital events
- NFT school memorabilia

### Privacy-First Marketing
- Less reliance on tracking
- First-party data importance
- Email list building (owned channel)
- Community engagement

### Authenticity
- User-generated content
- Raw, unpolished videos
- Student/parent voices
- Transparency

### Hyperlocal
- Neighborhood-specific messaging
- Micro-targeted ads
- Local SEO importance
- Community partnerships

### Preparation

- [ ] Start building AI-ready infrastructure
- [ ] Invest in video content
- [ ] Build first-party data (email, CRM)
- [ ] Test new platforms early
- [ ] Stay flexible and experimental

Future is about personalization, authenticity, and emerging platforms. Start experimenting now. Early winners will dominate.
