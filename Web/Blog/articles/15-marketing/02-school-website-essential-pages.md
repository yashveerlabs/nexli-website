## School Website: Essential Pages and Content

**Your website is often a parent's first impression of your school. It should answer key questions: Who are you? What's your education philosophy? What are your results? How do I enroll? This guide lists essential pages, what content each needs, and SEO best practices.**

---

## Essential Pages

### 1. Home Page
**Purpose:** First impression. Hook parents to explore.

**Content:**
- Tagline (who you are in 1 sentence)
- Hero image (welcoming, inclusive, action shot)
- Why choose us (3–5 key differentiators)
- Latest news/achievements (carousel or highlights)
- Quick links to admissions, academics, about

**Best practice:** Load in <3 seconds, mobile-responsive, clear CTA ("Apply Now", "Tour School")

---

### 2. About Us
**Purpose:** Tell your story.

**Content:**
- School history and mission
- Leadership bios (Principal, VP, key staff)
- Facilities (photo tour)
- Accreditations/recognitions
- Community involvement

---

### 3. Academics
**Purpose:** Explain your curriculum and approach.

**Content:**
- Curriculum overview (CBSE, NEP 2020, etc.)
- Teaching methodology
- Exam results (pass rate, topper achievements)
- Subject-wise highlights
- Co-curricular programs
- NEP 2020 HPC (if applicable)

---

### 4. Admissions
**Purpose:** Make enrollment easy.

**Content:**
- Admission timeline and deadlines
- Eligibility criteria (age, documents needed)
- Fee structure (transparency builds trust)
- How to apply (step-by-step)
- Frequently asked questions
- Contact form or application link

---

### 5. Faculty & Staff
**Purpose:** Build trust through team visibility.

**Content:**
- Staff directory (photos, qualifications, experience)
- Teacher spotlights (what do they specialize in?)
- Professional development (how are you investing in staff?)

---

### 6. Student Life
**Purpose:** Give parents a feel for the environment.

**Content:**
- Student testimonials (real quotes, photos)
- Daily schedule/timetable overview
- Clubs and activities
- Sports programs
- Student achievements (awards, recognitions)
- Photo gallery (events, learning moments)

---

### 7. Facilities & Infrastructure
**Purpose:** Reassure parents of quality environment.

**Content:**
- Campus tour (photo/video)
- Labs (science, computer, art)
- Library
- Sports grounds
- Dining and accommodation (for boarding schools)
- Medical clinic

---

### 8. Fees & Finance
**Purpose:** Transparency on costs.

**Content:**
- Fee structure (by class, itemized)
- Payment methods
- Discounts/scholarships available
- Financial aid process
- Bus fee (if applicable)
- Hostel fee (if applicable)

---

### 9. Contact Us
**Purpose:** Easy communication.

**Content:**
- School address, phone, email
- Office hours
- Contact form
- Map to school
- Social media links

---

### 10. Parent Portal (Login)
**Purpose:** Exclusive parent access.

**Content (after login):**
- Child's timetable, homework, marks
- Attendance record
- Fee payment status
- School announcements
- Teacher contact
- Leave application

---

## Additional Strategic Pages

**Blog:** Share updates, tips for parents, student spotlights
**FAQ:** Answer common questions (reduces email volume)
**Safety & Compliance:** Fire NOC, DPDP compliance statement, safeguarding policy
**Alumni:** Celebrate past students, community building
**Careers:** If hiring, attract good teachers

## FAQ

**Q1: Should our website have student photos?**  
A: Yes, with parental consent and DPDP compliance. Faces + names together create privacy risk. Use first names only, or face photos without names.

**Q2: How often should we update the website?**  
A: Critical info (admissions, fees): quarterly. News: weekly. Testimonials/photos: annually.

**Q3: Should we have a student portal?**  
A: Yes (for older students). Homework, marks, timetable—accessible 24/7.
