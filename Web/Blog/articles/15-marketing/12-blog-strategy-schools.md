## Blog Strategy for Schools: Content Marketing Excellence

**Blogs build authority, drive SEO traffic, and provide value to parents. A school blog answers parent questions, showcases expertise, and converts readers into leads. This guide covers strategy, content ideas, and publishing.**

---

## Blog Content Ideas

**Parent-Focused:**
- "How to Choose the Right School: 10 Factors"
- "Parent Involvement: Why It Matters"
- "Exam Stress: Tips for Supporting Your Child"
- "Understanding NEP 2020: What Parents Need to Know"

**Educational:**
- "CBSE Curriculum: What Your Child Learns"
- "STEM Education: Building Future Leaders"
- "Online Learning vs. Classroom: Pros and Cons"
- "Importance of Holistic Education"

**Student-Focused:**
- "Time Management Tips for Students"
- "Career Guidance: Finding Your Path"
- "Importance of Extracurriculars"
- "Study Skills That Work"

**School-Focused:**
- "Our Approach to Academics"
- "Student Achievement Spotlight: [Student Name]"
- "Behind-the-Scenes: A Day in Our Labs"
- "Our Commitment to Safety and Compliance"

---

## Publishing Schedule

**Frequency:** 1–2 posts per week (consistent > frequent)
**Length:** 800–2,000 words (longer content ranks better)
**Format:** Title, intro, 3–5 sections, conclusion, call-to-action

---

## SEO Optimization

- Keyword in title: "How to Choose a School"
- Keyword in H2: Use throughout
- Internal links: Link to admissions, academics pages
- Meta description: Compelling 155-char summary

---

## Blog Metrics

- Page views
- Time on page (should be >2 min)
- Bounce rate (should be <60%)
- Click-through (to admissions or other site pages)

**Goal:** 50+ qualified leads from blog per month
