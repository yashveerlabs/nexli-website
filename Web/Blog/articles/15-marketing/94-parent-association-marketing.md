# Parent Association as Marketing Asset: Leveraging Community Power

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Parent Association: Your Internal Marketing Team

Parent Association members are your school advocates. Leverage their enthusiasm.

Parent Association referrals convert at 50%+ rate (highest of all channels).

---

## Strategy

### Empower Parents
- Give them referral tools (brochures, links)
- Highlight school achievements
- Share talking points
- Recognize referring parents

### Communication
- Monthly parent association updates
- Feature success stories
- Share enrollment data
- Celebrate achievements together

### Events
- Parent-led open houses
- Parent testimonial events
- Parent-guided tours
- Parent networking events

### Recognition
- Thank you emails
- Feature in newsletter
- Small gifts/certificates
- Scholarships for referred children

### Measurement
- Referrals from parent association
- Conversion rate
- Number of involved parents
- Growth from this channel

### Referral Process
- Simple referral form
- Easy sharing (email, link, QR)
- Track referrals
- Follow up promptly
- Thank referring parents
