# Alumni Newsletter: Staying Connected After Graduation

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Alumni Newsletter: Long-Term Engagement

Alumni are your best brand ambassadors. A quarterly alumni newsletter keeps them connected, informed, and engaged.

Alumni who stay connected mentor current students, refer new families, and donate.

---

## Strategy

### Content
- Alumni achievements (college, career success)
- Alumni mentorship opportunities
- Alumni events and reunions
- Current school updates
- Impact of school on their success
- Giving/volunteer opportunities

### Frequency
Quarterly (once every 3 months)

### Distribution
- Email (primary)
- Alumni Facebook group (secondary)
- LinkedIn (optional, for professionals)
- WhatsApp group (if exists)

### Engagement
- Invite alumni to speak at school
- Feature alumni in parent newsletters
- Ask alumni to mentor current students
- Invite to open houses

### Lead Generation
Alumni testimonials in newsletters can drive new family inquiries.

### Measurement
- Open rate
- Click rate
- RSVP to events
- Mentorship sign-ups
- Donations
