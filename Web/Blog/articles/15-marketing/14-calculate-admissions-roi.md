## How to Calculate Admissions ROI: Marketing Spend to Enrollment

**Know your ROI: How much do you spend to acquire one student? This guide covers metrics, calculations, and optimization strategies.**

---

## Key Metrics

**Cost Per Lead (CPL):** How much to get one inquiry?
- Formula: Total marketing spend / Total leads
- Example: ₹1,00,000 / 20 leads = ₹5,000 CPL

**Conversion Rate:** % of leads who become students
- Formula: Enrolled students / Total leads
- Example: 5 enrollments / 20 leads = 25% conversion

**Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** How much per enrolled student?
- Formula: Total marketing spend / Enrolled students
- OR: CPL / Conversion rate
- Example: ₹5,000 / 0.25 = ₹20,000 CPA

**ROI:** Is it worth it?
- Formula: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100%
- Example: 1 student × ₹2,00,000 annual fee - ₹20,000 CPA = ₹1,80,000 / ₹20,000 = 800% ROI (excellent)

---

## Benchmark Analysis

**For every ₹1 spent, how much revenue do you get?**
- Target: ₹10–50 return (depending on fee)
- Example: ₹1 spent, ₹20 fee revenue = 20x ROI (healthy)

**Profitable CPA:**
- Rule of thumb: CPA should be <5% of annual fee
- If fee ₹2,00,000, CPA should be <₹10,000

---

## Tracking Setup

**Use UTM Parameters (for Google Ads & social):**
- Google Ads: Automatically tracked
- Social media ads: Add tracking URL
- Email campaigns: Track clicks

**Website analytics:**
- Google Analytics tracks traffic source
- Setup goal: "Inquiry submission" or "Application"
- See which source drives most conversions

**CRM/Admissions system:**
- Log every lead (source, date, status, outcome)
- Track from inquiry → application → enrollment
- Calculate conversion rate per source

---

## Optimization Strategies

**If CPL is too high:**
- Reduce bid amount (Google Ads)
- Improve ad relevance (better copy, targeting)
- Switch to organic channels (SEO, social organic)

**If conversion rate is low:**
- Improve landing page (clarity, mobile, CTA)
- Follow up faster (call leads within 24 hours)
- Better lead qualification (ask better questions upfront)

**If CPA is high:**
- Reduce marketing spend on low-ROI channels
- Double down on high-ROI channels
- Balance between immediate (ads) and long-term (SEO, referrals)

---

## Channel-Specific ROI

**Google Ads:** ₹3,000–8,000 CPA (immediate, measurable)
**Social Ads:** ₹2,000–6,000 CPA (brand building + leads)
**Referrals:** ₹0 CPA (organic word-of-mouth)
**SEO/Blog:** ₹0 CPA (slow start, compounding)
**Direct (parents find you):** ₹0 CPA (word-of-mouth, reputation)

---

## Annual ROI Calculation

**Example:**
- Annual marketing budget: ₹5,00,000
- Total leads: 100
- Conversion rate: 20%
- Enrolled students: 20
- Average annual fee: ₹2,00,000
- Revenue from these students (annual): 20 × ₹2,00,000 = ₹40,00,000
- Profit (year 1): ₹40,00,000 - ₹5,00,000 = ₹35,00,000 (700% ROI in year 1)
- Note: Students stay 5–12 years, so lifetime value is much higher
