# School Tours: Converting Prospects to Applications

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## School Tours: The Conversion Moment

80% of parents who tour convert to applicants. Tours are where interest becomes enrollment.

Tour experience determines if they enroll or go elsewhere.

---

## Strategy

### Before Tour
- Welcome email with details
- Directions and parking info
- Agenda
- What to expect
- Contact person

### During Tour (90 min)
- 10 min: Welcome by principal/staff
- 60 min: Campus exploration (classroom, labs, sports, art)
- 20 min: Q&A and discussion
- Capture contact for follow-up

### Best Practices
- Show real classes (not rehearsed)
- Let them see student interactions
- Highlight unique programs
- Address parent concerns
- Student guide can speak
- Provide brochure and next steps

### Timing
- Tour during school day (see real school)
- Multiple times per week
- By appointment (not random)

### Follow-Up
- Within 24 hours: Email thank you + application link
- Day 7: Call if no application
- Day 14: Final push

### Measurement
- Tours per month
- Conversion rate (tours to applications)
- Cost per enrollment from tours
