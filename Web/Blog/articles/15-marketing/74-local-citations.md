# Local Citations: Google, Justdial, Local Directory Listings

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Citations: Boost Local SEO

Citations are online mentions of your school name, address, phone (NAP) on directories.

Schools with 20+ citations rank 2x higher for local keywords.

---

## Strategy

### Major Directories
- Google Business Profile
- Justdial (most important in India)
- Indiastack
- Local city guides
- Education portals
- Mom blogs and school directories

### Citation Consistency
Ensure name, address, phone IDENTICAL across all directories.
(Even small differences hurt rankings)

### What to Include
- School name (exact)
- Complete address
- Phone number
- Website
- Hours
- Categories (Primary School, Secondary School)
- Photos
- Description

### Claim Existing Listings
- Search "[School Name] Justdial"
- Claim profile
- Update information
- Add photos

### Add to New Directories
- List in 10-15 directories
- Ensure consistent NAP
- Fill out all fields
- Add photos

### Local Backlinks
- Partner directory listings
- Education portal links
- Local business listings

### Measurement
- Track citation count (use Ubersuggest or Semrush)
- Monitor local rankings
- Track phone calls from directories
