## Facebook for Schools: Best Practices and Engagement

**Facebook is the most popular social media among parents (age 35+). A strong Facebook presence builds community, drives admissions, and provides parent support. This guide covers strategy, content, engagement, and ads.**

---

## Facebook Setup

**Business Page (not Personal Page):**
- School name, address, phone, hours
- Logo and cover photo
- "Call to Action" button (Apply Now, Contact Us)
- Complete About section (mission, brief description)

**Optimize Profile:**
- Profile photo: Logo or school building
- Cover photo: Welcoming, student-centered image
- Bio: 1-line school tagline ("Best CBSE school in [City]")

---

## Content Strategy

**Post Mix:**
- Academic updates (30%): Exam results, achievements
- School life (40%): Events, activities, student spotlights
- Announcements (20%): Admissions, parent meetings
- Community (10%): Values, diversity, social impact

**Posting Frequency:** 4–5 posts per week

**Content Types:**
- Photos (highest engagement)
- Videos (school tour, testimonials, event highlights)
- Stories (behind-the-scenes, casual updates)
- Events (admissions dates, parent meetings)

---

## Engagement Best Practices

**Respond to Comments:** Within 24 hours, friendly tone
**Encourage UGC:** "Tag us in your school memories"
**Go Live:** During school events (open house, assembly)
**Use Polls:** "What's your favorite school program?" → engagement + feedback
**Hashtags:** #SchoolName, #StudentLife, #SchoolCommunity

---

## Facebook Ads for Admissions

**Ad Objective:** Website conversions (lead inquiries)

**Audience:** Parents age 35–55, interested in education, location-targeted (city/nearby areas)

**Budget:** ₹500–2,000/day (similar to Google Ads)

**Ad Format:**
- Image: School photo or achievement
- Text: "Admissions open for 2026–27. Best CBSE school in [City]. Apply now."
- CTA button: "Apply Now" or "Learn More"

**Expected:** ₹2,000–5,000 cost per lead

---

## Metrics to Track

**Reach:** How many saw your post?
**Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares (target: 5%+ engagement rate)
**Click-Through:** How many clicked link to website?
**Conversion:** Leads from Facebook ads
**Follower growth:** +100–200 followers/month is healthy

---

## Common Mistakes

**Inactive page:** Posts once a month → audience forgets you
**Only sales:** "Enroll now!" every post → parents unfollow
**Inconsistent branding:** Different tone, visuals → confusing
**No response:** Parents comment, you ignore → poor reputation
**Fake positivity:** Never address concerns → loss of trust

## FAQ

**Q1: Should we run Facebook Ads?**  
A: Yes, if budget >₹10K/month. Cost per lead: ₹2,000–5,000. ROI is strong for admissions.

**Q2: How do we handle negative comments?**  
A: Respond professionally. "We're sorry to hear. DM us to discuss." Take it offline.

**Q3: Can we post student photos?**  
A: Yes, with parental consent. Use first names only, faces + names together create privacy risk.
