# Press Release: Writing and Distributing Press Releases

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Press Release: Get Into Local News

A well-written press release gets your school in local news. News coverage is credible, reaches new families.

One press release = 20-50 qualified inquiries from media coverage.

---

## Strategy

### Press Release Topics
- Major award or recognition
- New program launch
- Student achievement highlight
- Leadership announcement
- Community initiative
- Partnership announcement

### Structure
- Headline (compelling, newsworthy)
- Dateline (City, Date)
- Opening (Who, what, when, where, why)
- Body (Key facts, details)
- Quote (Principal or staff)
- Closing (About school)
- Contact info

### Distribution
- Local journalists (email personally)
- News outlets (newspaper, online, TV)
- Press release platforms (PIB, Prweb)
- Social media

### Timing
- 1-2 press releases per term
- Newsworthy events only
- Send 1-2 weeks before announcement

### Journalists to Contact
- Local reporters (education beat)
- School education correspondents
- Community news editors
- Parenting bloggers

### Follow-Up
- Call journalists after sending
- Offer interviews/photos
- Make it easy for them to cover

### Measurement
- Media mentions (track)
- Article reach (estimate audience)
- Inquiries from coverage
- Social media shares
