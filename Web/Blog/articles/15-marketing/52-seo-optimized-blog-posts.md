## Blog SEO: The Ultimate Long-Term Investment

A blog post ranking #1 on Google for "best CBSE schools in [City]" generates 10–20 inquiries per month. For years.

Cost to rank: 2 hours writing + ₹0.
ROI: 10 inquiries/month × 12 months × ₹1L value = ₹1.2Cr over lifetime of that post.

---

## Strategy: SEO-Optimized Blogging

### 1. **Keyword Research**
Use Google Keyword Planner or Ubersuggest.

Find keywords with:
- High search volume (1,000+ searches/month)
- Low competition (easy to rank)
- High intent (parents searching, not just random)

Example: "CBSE board exam preparation" (10K searches/month, medium competition)

### 2. **Keyword Placement**
Include keyword in:
- Title: "CBSE Board Exam Preparation: 30-Day Study Plan for Success"
- H1 (main heading): Once
- H2 (subheadings): 2–3 times
- First 100 words: Work it naturally
- Body: 1–2% keyword density (not overstuffed)
- Meta description: 155 chars

### 3. **Content Length**
Posts should be 1,500–2,000 words.

Longer posts rank better (more comprehensive, more detailed).

### 4. **Internal Linking**
Link to 2–3 other blog posts within the article.

"See our guide on [blog post]" (link)

This helps Google understand your site structure.

### 5. **External Linking**
Link to 2–3 credible external sources.

Link to CBSE website, research papers, education blogs.

Shows you've researched thoroughly.

### 6. **Formatting for Readability**
- Short paragraphs (2–3 sentences)
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Subheadings (H2, H3)
- Bold key points
- 1 image per 300 words
- Tables or infographics

Easy to scan = better user experience = better ranking.

### 7. **Mobile Optimization**
Post must load fast on mobile.

- Compress images
- Minimize CSS/JS
- Remove ads/popups
- Vertical layout, not horizontal

Test on real phone.

### 8. **Meta Description**
Write 155-character summary:

"Learn how to prepare for CBSE Class 10 board exams in just 30 days. Expert study strategies, time management tips, and real student success stories."

This appears in Google results. Compelling description = higher click-through rate.

### 9. **Publishing**
- Publish on your blog
- Add to sitemap
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Wait 2–4 weeks for indexing
- Share on social media
- Email to list

### 10. **Measurement**
Track in Google Search Console:
- Keyword rankings (what position you're ranking at)
- Search impressions (how many people saw your listing)
- Clicks (how many clicked through)
- Click-through rate
- Average ranking position

Goal: Rank in top 3 for target keywords.
