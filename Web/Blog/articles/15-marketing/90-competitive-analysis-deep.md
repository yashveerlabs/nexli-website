# Competitive Analysis Deep Dive: Understanding Your Market Position

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Competitive Analysis: Win the Market

Know your competitors inside-out. Identify gaps. Fill them with your positioning.

Schools with deep competitive analysis win 20% more market share.

---

## Analysis Framework

### Competitor Selection
- Identify 5-8 direct competitors (same board, location, segment)
- Analyze each systematically

### Website Analysis
- Positioning/messaging
- Unique value propositions
- Program offerings
- Pricing visibility
- CTA effectiveness
- Mobile optimization
- SEO (keywords they rank for)

### Social Media
- Posting frequency
- Engagement rate
- Follower count
- Content themes
- Audience demographics

### Reviews & Reputation
- Google rating
- Review count
- Common themes in reviews (praise and complaints)
- Response to reviews

### Pricing Strategy
- Fee structure
- Discounts offered
- Scholarship policies
- Program pricing

### Marketing Channels
- Where they advertise (Google, Facebook, outdoor)
- Sponsorships and partnerships
- Press coverage
- Events and open houses

### Strengths & Weaknesses
Document competitor:
- Strengths (what they do well)
- Weaknesses (what they're missing)
- Opportunities (gaps you can fill)
- Threats (capabilities you need to match)

### Your Differentiation
Based on analysis:
- What are you better at?
- What market gaps can you fill?
- What's your unique positioning?
- How will you win?

### Measurement
- Track competitor changes quarterly
- Monitor their ads and content
- Track their inquiries/growth
- Evaluate your own competitive position
