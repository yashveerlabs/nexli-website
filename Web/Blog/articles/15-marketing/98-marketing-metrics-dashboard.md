# Marketing Metrics Dashboard: Tracking Performance and ROI

**Published:** June 19, 2026 | **Category:** School Marketing | **Read time:** 5 min

---

## Marketing Dashboard: Measure Everything

You can't manage what you don't measure. Build a dashboard to track all marketing metrics.

Schools with dashboards improve ROI by 30% annually.

---

## Key Metrics

### Traffic
- Website visitors/month
- Visitors by source (organic, paid, social, referral)
- Pageviews
- Time on page
- Bounce rate

### Leads
- Inquiries/month
- Cost per inquiry
- Inquiries by source
- Lead quality (% converting to tours)

### Engagement
- Email open rate
- Email click rate
- Social media followers
- Social engagement rate
- Video views

### Conversion
- Tours booked/month
- Applications submitted/month
- Enrollments/month
- Cost per enrollment
- ROI by channel

### Brand
- Website ranking for keywords
- Brand search volume
- Media mentions
- Awards and recognition

### Dashboard Display
- Monthly view (current performance)
- Trend view (3-month, 12-month)
- By channel breakdown
- Vs. targets (on track, below, above)

### Tools
- Google Analytics (traffic)
- Email platform (email metrics)
- CRM (inquiries, applications, enrollments)
- Google Search Console (SEO)
- Social media analytics (social metrics)
- Excel or Looker (consolidate)

### Review Cadence
- Weekly review (tactical adjustments)
- Monthly review (report to leadership)
- Quarterly review (strategic decisions)

---

## Implementation

- [ ] Select metrics to track
- [ ] Build dashboard (Excel or Looker)
- [ ] Set targets for each metric
- [ ] Automate data collection
- [ ] Weekly review meetings
- [ ] Monthly reporting to leadership

Dashboard gives visibility. Visibility enables optimization. Optimization drives results.
