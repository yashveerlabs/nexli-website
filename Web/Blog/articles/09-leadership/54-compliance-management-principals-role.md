# Compliance Management: Principal's Role

Compliance with laws and regulations is mandatory. Systematic oversight ensures adherence.

**Regulatory Knowledge** - Education laws, FERPA, ADA, Title IX, state requirements, local policies, labor laws, financial regulations, ongoing education on changes.

**Policy Development** - School policies aligned to regulations, clear procedures, staff training, updates as needed, board approval, documentation, accessibility.

**Documentation** - Records maintained, evidence of compliance, audit trails, secure storage, privacy protected, accessibility for review.

**Reporting** - Required reports submitted timely, accurate data, compliance documentation, financial reports, assessment data, to government/district.

**Monitoring** - Regular compliance checks, self-audits, policies followed, procedures implemented, staff adherence, quick correction if issues.

**Staff Training** - Mandatory training on compliance issues, FERPA, Title IX, mandated reporting, harassment prevention, updated regularly, documentation.

**Internal Audit** - Regular review of compliance status, identify gaps, correction plans, resource allocation, board communication, continuous improvement.

**External Audit** - Independent review if applicable, findings addressed, improvement plans, follow-up verification, transparency.

**Legal Support** - Legal counsel on complex issues, contract review, liability protection, disputes resolved, proactive legal guidance.

**Communication** - Families understand policies, staff understand procedures, transparency, community confidence, accessible information.

Compliance isn't burdensome if systems are in place. Make it automatic and standard operating procedure.
