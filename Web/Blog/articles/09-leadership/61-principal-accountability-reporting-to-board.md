# Principal Accountability: Reporting to Board

Clear accountability demonstrates leadership effectiveness and builds board confidence.

**Monthly Reports** - Student achievement progress, financial status, operational updates, challenges addressed, progress toward goals, attendance data, safety incidents, staff changes, community engagement.

**Annual Evaluation** - Performance against goals, achievement results, budget management, staff development, school culture, strategic initiatives, innovation, external recognition, areas for improvement, goals for next year.

**Strategic Goals Progress** - Quarterly review of multi-year goals, milestones achieved, on track or adjusted, resources sufficient, barriers addressed, evidence of progress.

**Financial Accountability** - Budget vs. actual spending, cash position, financial reserves, major decisions, compliance status, audit results, risk management.

**Community Feedback** - Parent surveys, staff satisfaction, student engagement, community perception, reputation trends, areas needing improvement, celebration of strengths.

**Professional Growth** - Professional development pursued, leadership skills, new initiatives led, conference attendance, publications or presentations, continued learning.

Documentation and transparency ensure mutual accountability between principal and board.
