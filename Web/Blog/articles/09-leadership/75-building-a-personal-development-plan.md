# Building a Personal Development Plan

Personal growth as principal directly impacts school. Intentional development ensures growth.

**Self-Assessment** - Strengths and weaknesses honestly, feedback from others, skills needed for role, growth areas identified, realistic assessment.

**Goal-Setting** - 1-3 development goals, specific and measurable, ambitious but achievable, aligned to school needs, personal aspirations.

**Plan Development** - How will you develop? Learning activities, timeline, resources needed, accountability, milestone tracking, success criteria.

**Learning Activities** - Coaching, courses, reading, conferences, mentoring, peer learning, experience, multiple methods, tailored to learning style.

**Progress Monitoring** - Regular review of progress, milestones achieved, adjustments made, celebrations, accountability, course correction if needed.

**Support System** - Coach or mentor, peer support, family support, organizational support, resources provided, enabling success.

**Integration** - Apply learning to work, practice new skills, implement changes, share learning, model behavior, visible growth.

**Annual Review** - Assess progress, celebrate achievement, reflect on learning, new goals for next year, continuous growth trajectory.

Intentional personal development makes you a more effective leader and role model for staff.
