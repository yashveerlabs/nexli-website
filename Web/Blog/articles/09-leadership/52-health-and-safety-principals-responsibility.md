# Health and Safety: Principal's Responsibility

Safe, healthy school environment is non-negotiable. You ensure systems protect everyone.

**Emergency Procedures** - Evacuation, lockdown, shelter-in-place, medical emergency. Procedures documented, staff trained, regular drills, incident response plan.

**Health Protocols** - Illness policies, contagious disease screening, vaccination requirements, hand sanitizing stations, health screening programs, communicable disease response.

**Mental Health Support** - Counseling services, crisis response, suicide prevention, trauma-informed practices, staff mental health, student emotional wellbeing.

**Bullying Prevention** - Clear policies, reporting procedures, investigation, consequences, prevention programming, restorative practices, safe community.

**Substance Abuse** - Drug-free policy, prevention education, testing if appropriate, intervention programs, treatment resources, education for all.

**Violence Prevention** - Threat assessment, conflict resolution, de-escalation training, safe spaces, monitoring and reporting, rapid response.

**Physical Safety** - Secure facilities, visitor screening, access control, playground safety, equipment maintained, injury prevention.

**Hazardous Materials** - Safe storage, MSDS available, proper handling, spill cleanup, exposure prevention, staff training.

**Liability Management** - Insurance adequate, incident documentation, risk management, legal consultation, safety culture.

Health and safety are foundational. No learning happens in unsafe environments. Make safety your top priority.
