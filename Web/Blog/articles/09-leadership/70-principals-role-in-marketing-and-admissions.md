# Principal's Role in Marketing and Admissions

Enrollment depends on effective marketing and admissions. Principal leads enrollment growth strategy.

**Define School's Brand** - Unique value proposition, mission clarity, target market definition, differentiation, messaging, positioning, school identity.

**Marketing Strategy** - Website, social media, events, word-of-mouth, advertising, partnerships, media coverage, visible presence, recruitment target.

**Admissions Process** - Clear procedures, timeline, requirements, communication, family tours, application process, decision timeline, efficient operations.

**Recruitment** - Target marketing to desired populations, recruitment events, school visits, testimonials, materials appealing, competitive advantages highlighted.

**Enrollment Management** - Enrollment forecasting, target numbers, pipeline management, lead tracking, conversion rates, enrollment goals achievement.

**Retention** - Keep families satisfied, communication, engagement, listening to concerns, responding to needs, family satisfaction, word-of-mouth retention.

**Principal Leadership** - Visible role in enrollment, story telling, community presence, relationship building, prospect meetings, parent tours, personal connection.

**Evaluation** - Enrollment goals achieved?, Growth trajectory?, Retention rates?, Satisfaction levels?, Marketing ROI?, Continuous improvement.

Effective marketing and admissions ensure school thrives with engaged families and students aligned to mission.
