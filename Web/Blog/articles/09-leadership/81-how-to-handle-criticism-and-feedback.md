# How to Handle Criticism and Feedback

Criticism is inevitable. How you handle it determines your growth and effectiveness.

**Listen First** - Don't defend immediately, hear fully, understand their perspective, what are they saying, ask clarifying questions, listen to understand.

**Separate Person from Feedback** - Their criticism doesn't define you, may be valid even if unhappy, focus on content not delivery, don't take personally, professional response.

**Assess Validity** - Is there truth in criticism? Is it fair? Even unfair criticism has grain of truth sometimes. Honest self-assessment, growth opportunity.

**Response Time** - Don't respond in anger, take time to process, reflect, consider, respond thoughtfully, professional demeanor, calm interaction.

**Gracious Response** - Thank them for feedback, acknowledge perspective, explain if needed, don't be defensive, show respect for their view, open-minded appearance.

**Take Action** - If feedback valid, act on it, change behavior, demonstrate improvement, follow-up conversation, show it mattered, continuous improvement.

**Dismiss if Unwarranted** - Not all criticism is valid, discern carefully, may need to push back respectfully, defend position professionally, disagree respectfully.

**Share with Supporters** - Talk to mentor, peer, coach, process feedback, get perspective, support for growth, emotional processing.

**Model It for Others** - Show how to take criticism gracefully, growth mindset, continuous improvement, humble leadership, learning orientation, model behavior.

Leaders who learn from feedback grow constantly. Those who resist criticism stagnate.
