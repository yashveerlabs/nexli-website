## Part 6: Monitoring and Accountability

**Quarterly Reviews**
- Leadership team review of progress
- Data analysis and trend identification
- Adjustment recommendations
- Communication to staff and board

**Annual Assessment**
- Comprehensive review of year's progress
- Achievement of annual goals
- Lessons learned
- Adjustments for next year
- Public reporting of progress

**Accountability Structure**
- Progress toward plan goals included in performance evaluations
- Leadership team members assigned responsibility for each priority
- Monthly progress discussions at leadership meetings
- Public dashboard of progress indicators

## Part 8: Plan Adaptation

**Annual Review Process**
- Full team review of plan relevance
- Assessment of progress toward goals
- Environmental scan for new challenges/opportunities
- Recommendation for adjustments

**Trigger Points for Major Revision**
- Significant leadership change
- Major environmental shift (pandemic, regulation change, economic change)
- Failure to achieve priority outcomes after two years
- Emergence of critical new challenge or opportunity

This template provides structure while leaving flexibility for your school's unique context. Use it to create a living document that guides your school toward excellence.
