# How to Prepare for School Inspections

Inspections assess compliance and quality. Preparation shows readiness and professionalism.

**Know Requirements** - Inspection standards, compliance expectations, rubrics, what will be observed, documentation needed, areas of focus.

**Self-Assessment** - Before inspection, assess yourself against standards, identify gaps, address issues, correction plans for gaps, readiness check.

**Documentation** - Organize files, evidence compiled, policies documented, procedures clear, records organized, quick access to information.

**Staff Preparation** - Brief staff on what inspectors will see, expectations for interactions, behavior professionalism, how to discuss school.

**Facilities** - Clean and organized, safety visible, resources evident, technology functional, welcoming environment, pride in spaces.

**Processes** - Demonstrate systems for teaching, assessment, feedback, professional development, meeting minutes, data, improvement evidence.

**Communication** - Prepare talking points, school story clear, strategic priorities explained, evidence of improvement, mission and vision evident.

**Day-of** - Welcoming hosts, transparent access, honest answers, confidence in systems, pride in school, professional demeanor.

**Follow-up** - Address any findings, improvement plans if needed, communicate with stakeholders, celebrate strengths, continuous improvement.

Inspections are opportunities to demonstrate your school's quality and commitment to excellence.
