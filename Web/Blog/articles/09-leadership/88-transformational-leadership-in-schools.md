# Transformational Leadership in Schools

Transformational leaders inspire others to pursue shared vision and exceed expectations.

**Inspirational Vision** - Paint compelling picture of future, why it matters, hope and possibility, connect to mission, inspire commitment, shared vision.

**Intellectual Stimulation** - Challenge assumptions, encourage creativity, question status quo, innovation welcomed, intellectual engagement, growth mindset.

**Individual Consideration** - Recognize individual strengths, personalized support, mentoring, development, individual attention, respect and care for people.

**Modeling Behavior** - Demonstrate values you espouse, integrity and commitment, visible engagement, authenticity, walking the walk, credibility.

**Charisma and Presence** - Positive energy, confidence, presence, engagement, enthusiasm contagious, people drawn to follow, inspiring presence.

**Empowerment** - Delegate meaningful work, trust staff, autonomy within parameters, shared decision-making, leadership distributed, ownership and commitment.

**High Standards** - Excellence expected, accountability, continuous improvement, rigorous goals, supported to achieve, belief in capability.

**Relationship Building** - Genuine connections, care for people, listen to concerns, understand individual needs, community of belonging, trust developed.

**Change Leadership** - Lead transformation, new direction, why change matters, support through change, manage resistance, vision realized.

**Results and Impact** - Accomplish goals, results visible, impact meaningful, success celebrated, momentum building, transformational change evident.

Transformational leaders create schools where people want to work and students thrive.
