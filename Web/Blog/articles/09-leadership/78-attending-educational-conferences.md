# Attending Educational Conferences

Conferences provide learning, networking, and inspiration. Make them worthwhile.

**Selecting Conferences** - Choose aligned to priorities, quality sessions offered, networking opportunities, budget feasible, travel manageable, value return.

**Planning** - Schedule sessions in advance, identify must-attend speakers, plan networking, arrange logistics, communicate with staff, set learning goals.

**Active Participation** - Attend sessions, take notes, engage with presenters, network actively, business cards ready, connections made, learning captured.

**Networking** - Meet other principals, exchange information, find mentors/peers, business relationships, learning opportunities, community building, future collaboration.

**Learning Capture** - Detailed notes, key takeaways documented, photos of resources, contact information captured, reflection on applications, learning organized.

**Sharing Learning** - Share with staff, present at faculty meetings, key learning highlighted, implementation planning, collective learning, return on investment demonstrated.

**Vendor and Product Exploration** - Review new products/services, compare options, gather information, future purchasing decisions, innovation discovery.

**Following Up** - Contacts nurtured, promised follow-up completed, new connections strengthened, learning implemented, conference impact visible.

Conferences renew thinking and provide national perspective that strengthens school leadership.
