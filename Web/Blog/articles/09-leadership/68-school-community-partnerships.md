# School Community Partnerships

Partnerships expand school's reach and resources. Strategic partnerships enhance mission.

**Identify Potential Partners** - Local businesses, nonprofits, government agencies, universities, faith organizations, community groups, mutual interest assessment.

**Develop Partnerships** - Clear mutual benefit, formal agreements, roles defined, communication plan, evaluation process, resource allocation, shared goals.

**Types of Partnerships** - Program support, mentorship, internships, donations, volunteers, expertise sharing, facility access, funding, professional development.

**Effective Implementation** - Staff trained on partnerships, volunteer management, student participation, quality control, appreciation for partners, evaluation and feedback.

**Communication** - Regular updates to partners, recognition and appreciation, results shared, challenges discussed, adjustment as needed, relationship building.

**Sustainability** - Long-term planning, continued funding, renewed commitment, adaptation to changes, benefits maintained, partnership evolution.

**Documentation** - Formal agreements, roles and responsibilities, evaluation criteria, communication schedule, exit strategy if needed, accountability.

Community partnerships bring resources, expertise, and opportunities that schools cannot provide alone.
