# Principal Certifications and Qualifications

Quality principals require education and certification. Invest in your qualifications.

**Master's Degree** - Educational Leadership, Administration, Management. Foundation for principal work. Required in most states. Advanced knowledge and credential.

**Principal Certification** - State-required certification. Demonstrates knowledge of leadership. Renewal requirements. Professional credentials. Required to serve as principal.

**Specialized Certifications** - Special education administration, multilingual leadership, instructional leadership, finance, human resources, specialized expertise.

**Professional Organizations** - NAESP, state associations, local organizations, networking, professional development, resources, community of practice.

**Licenses and Credentials** - Teaching license often required first step. Administrative license for principal role. Continuing education for renewal.

**Qualifications Development** - Intentionally pursue development, certificates and training, expertise areas, specialized knowledge, competitive positioning.

**Credential Maintenance** - Meet renewal requirements, continuing education, documentation, timely renewal, credential validity, professional standards.

**Competitive Advantage** - Advanced qualifications strengthen candidacy for positions, advancement, compensation, respect from peers, professional standing.

Strong qualifications demonstrate commitment to profession and continuous learning.
