# Alumni Relations: Principal's Role

Alumni are school ambassadors and potential donors. Build relationships intentionally.

**Stay Connected** - Alumni events, reunions, newsletters, social media, contact list maintained, regular communication, community building.

**Engagement** - Invite back to school, speaking opportunities, mentorship of current students, career support, leadership roles, meaningful involvement.

**Fundraising** - Alumni donations fund scholarships, programs, capital projects, donors recognized, involvement in development, major gift cultivation.

**Advocacy** - Alumni advocate for school, word-of-mouth recruitment, community presence, business partnerships, referrals, reputation building.

**Mentorship** - Alumni mentor students, career exploration, networking opportunities, life lessons, role modeling, real-world connection.

**Celebration** - Celebrate alumni achievements, awards, recognitions, pride in alumni, community bragging rights, school legacy continuation.

**Retention** - Alumni feel connected, continue engagement, give back, create community, lifetime relationship, school as anchor.

**Systems** - Alumni database maintained, contact information current, engagement tracking, communication regular, systematic approach.

Alumni are school's extended family. Invest in relationships that benefit current and future students.
