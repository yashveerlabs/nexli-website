# Principal as Community Leader

Principals are community leaders beyond school walls. Your influence shapes community.

**Visibility** - Be present in community, attend events, visible leadership, community recognizes you, presence matters, connection building, relationships.

**Advocacy** - Advocate for schools, education importance, student needs, policy positions, community education, thought leadership, speak up.

**Partnerships** - Business partnerships, government relationships, nonprofit collaboration, community organization connections, mutual benefit, networking.

**Service** - Service organizations, volunteer work, community involvement, give back, model citizenship, inspire others, community contribution.

**Communication** - Share school success stories, media presence, social media, public relations, education of community, transparency.

**Collaboration** - Work with other community leaders, collective impact, shared challenges, community improvement, partnership approach, leadership development.

**Influence** - Use position responsibly, model behavior, ethical leadership, integrity demonstrated, community respect, positive influence.

Principal's role extends beyond school building into community leadership and influence.
