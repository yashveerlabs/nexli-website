# Facility Management: Principal's Oversight

Quality facilities support learning. Your oversight ensures well-maintained, safe, clean, functional buildings.

**Maintenance Planning** - Preventive schedule developed, major repairs planned, capital projects, budget allocated, contractors managed, quality standards, regular assessment.

**Daily Operations** - Building opens/closes safely, utilities function, cleaning daily, minor issues addressed quickly, temperature controlled, aesthetic appeal.

**Safety Inspection** - Regular building walk-throughs, safety hazards identified and removed, emergency exits clear, fire extinguishers available, OSHA compliance.

**Custodial Staff** - Well-trained team, clear expectations, equipment and supplies adequate, performance monitored, appreciation expressed, professional development.

**Emergency Preparedness** - Evacuation plans, lockdown procedures, emergency supplies, first aid stations, staff trained, regular drills, incident response.

**Accessibility** - ADA compliance, accessible entry/bathrooms/classrooms, elevators functional, accommodations for disabilities, inclusive facilities.

**Capital Planning** - Multi-year facility plan, major projects prioritized, funding sources identified, construction managed, minimal disruption.

**Budget Oversight** - Facilities budget managed, preventive maintenance prioritized over emergency repairs, efficiency investments, cost control.

**Grounds** - Landscaping maintained, parking functional, exterior appearance, weather damage addressed, seasonal preparation.

Facilities matter because students and staff spend 6+ hours daily there. Quality facilities communicate that learning matters and students matter.
