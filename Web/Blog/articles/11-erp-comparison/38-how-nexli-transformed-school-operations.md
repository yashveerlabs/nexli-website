## How Nexli Addresses the Operational Challenges Schools Face

**School administration in India involves managing hundreds or thousands of students, dozens of staff, complex fee structures, compliance requirements across multiple regulatory bodies, and real-time communication with parents. Most schools manage these with a mix of spreadsheets, paper registers, and disconnected software. Nexli was built to unify these operations into a single system designed specifically for Indian K-12 schools.**

---

## The Problem: Fragmented School Operations

The typical Indian school before unified ERP adoption looks like this:

**Attendance:** Class teachers mark registers by hand. At the end of the week, someone compiles the data into Excel. Month-end attendance reports are manually calculated. Students near the 75% threshold are identified weeks too late.

**Fees:** The accounts team maintains fee ledgers in Tally or Excel. Parents call the school to know their balance. The Principal cannot see current collection rates without calling the accountant. Defaulters are followed up sporadically.

**Academic records:** Each teacher maintains their own marks sheet. Report cards are assembled by hand at exam time. Errors are discovered after printing. NEP 2020 portfolio requirements are tracked in physical files.

**Compliance:** CBSE LOC submission is compiled from class-by-class lists. UDISE+ data is re-entered from registers. DPDP parental consent is not systematically obtained.

**Communication:** Circulars are printed and sent home in bags (or not sent at all). Parents have no real-time visibility into their child's attendance or fee status.

This fragmentation is not a school management failure, it is what happens when each operational area uses a different tool without a central system.

## How Nexli Addresses Each Area

### Attendance

Nexli makes daily and period-wise attendance a one-tap operation on mobile. Class teachers see their section's roster on login; a single tap marks each student. The system tracks attendance from entry; at 80%, parents are automatically alerted. At 75%, the system flags the student for administrative follow-up. The Principal sees section-by-section attendance health on their dashboard every morning.

### Fee Management

Fee structures are configured in Nexli once per year. Each student's fee obligation is tracked automatically. When a payment is recorded (cash, cheque, or online via Razorpay), the receipt is generated immediately. Parents see their balance in the parent portal. The accounts team runs defaulter reports by class, by installment, or across the school in seconds. RTE quota students are tracked separately with reimbursement claims logged.

### Academic Records

Marks are entered by subject teachers directly into Nexli after each assessment. The system calculates grades automatically using the configured grading scale. NEP 2020 Holistic Progress Card indicators are tracked alongside traditional marks. Report cards are auto-populated and available for review by the Class Teacher and Principal before printing. Digital report cards can be sent to parents without printing.

### Compliance

The compliance calendar alerts staff about upcoming CBSE LOC submission, UDISE+ window, fire NOC renewal, POSH annual report, and other regulatory deadlines. CBSE LOC exports in the prescribed format. DPDP Act compliance is built-in, parental consent is OTP-verified and stored, with the DPO dashboard tracking who accessed what data.

### Communication

Circulars are published in Nexli and reach parents through the parent portal and push notifications. Teachers can send class-level announcements. Parents receive attendance alerts and fee reminders automatically without staff effort.
