## Quick Setup Guide

### UPI Acceptance

**Steps:**
1. Partner with Razorpay/Cashfree
2. Provide school bank account details
3. Get UPI ID (e.g., nexli@razorpay) or QR code
4. Display QR at fee counter or include in invoices

**Time to Setup:** 1–2 days
**Cost:** Free to ₹500/month depending on transaction volume
**Success Rate:** 99%+ (most reliable)

### Net Banking Acceptance

**Steps:**
1. Payment gateway handles it (Razorpay, Cashfree, etc.)
2. Parents redirected to their bank's portal
3. School receives confirmation

**Time to Setup:** 1–2 days (included in gateway setup)
**Cost:** 1–3% commission per transaction
**Success Rate:** 95% (some failures due to OTP, timeouts)

### Card Acceptance

**Steps:**
1. Gateway processes cards securely
2. Parents enter card details
3. One-time payment confirmation

**Time to Setup:** 1–2 days
**Cost:** 2–3% commission per transaction
**Success Rate:** 95% (some failures due to fraud checks, insufficient funds)

## Recommended Setup

**Tier 1: UPI (Primary)**
- Zero friction, instant, free/cheap
- Display QR at fee counter
- Include in all fee communications

**Tier 2: Net Banking**
- For parents without UPI
- Good fallback option

**Tier 3: Cards**
- For parents outside India or high-value transactions
- Higher cost, but covers edge cases

## At-School Payment Counter

**Physical Setup:**
- Install tablet/mobile at fee counter
- Integrate with payment gateway
- Parent scans QR on invoice → Instant payment

**OR**

- Display school's UPI QR on wall
- Parent scans with their phone
- Confirmation displayed on counter device

**OR**

- School collects via Point-of-Sale (POS) machine
- Integrates with payment gateway
- Instant reconciliation

## FAQ

**Q1: Which payment method is best for schools to accept?**
A: UPI is the best primary option, instant, zero-friction, and free or very low cost. Net banking and cards should be offered as fallback options so no parent is excluded.

**Q2: How long does it take for a school to start accepting UPI?**
A: 1–2 days. Partner with Razorpay or Cashfree, provide bank details, get a UPI ID or QR code, and you're live.

**Q3: Do schools need to handle PCI compliance for card payments?**
A: No. The payment gateway handles PCI compliance. Schools only need to ensure they collect proper DPDP Act consent from parents for payment processing.

**Q4: What happens if a parent's UPI payment fails?**
A: The parent is not charged. Nexli marks the payment as failed and the parent can retry through any other available method.

**Q5: Can parents choose their preferred payment method?**
A: Yes. When Nexli generates a payment link, parents see all available methods, UPI, net banking, and cards, and choose what works best for them.
